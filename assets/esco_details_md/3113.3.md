Description
Battery maintenance technicians are responsible for the maintenance and repair of the
equipment used in the production of batteries. They work in battery manufacturing plants
and are responsible for ensuring that the equipment is in good working order and is able to
produce high-quality batteries. They need to have a strong understanding of mechanical,
electrical, and control systems, as well as experience with maintenance and repair of
manufacturing equipment.
