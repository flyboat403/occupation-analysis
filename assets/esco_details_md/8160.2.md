Description
Baking operators tend automatic reels or conveyor-type ovens to bake bread, pastries and
other bakery products. They interpret work orders to determine the products and the
quantities to be baked. They set the operational speed of conveyors, baking times, and
temperatures. They supervise the baking process and maintain oven operations in control.
