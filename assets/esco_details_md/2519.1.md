Description
Data quality specialists review organisation's data for accuracy, recommend enhancements
to record systems and data acquisition processes and assess referential and historical
integrity of data. They also develop documents and maintain data quality goals and
standards and oversee an organisation's data privacy policy and monitor compliance of
data flows against data quality standards.
Scope note
Excludes people performing managerial and development activities.
