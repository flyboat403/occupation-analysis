Description
Speech and language therapists focus on the aetiology, assessment, diagnosis, treatment,
and prevention of communication and swallowing disorders in people of all ages in order to
help them maintain, promote, improve, initiate, or recover the ability to communicate both
verbally and nonverbally. They address the development of language, speech, vocal and
hearing functions, and disorders and disabilities in the cranial, facial, and oral area.
