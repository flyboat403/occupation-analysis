Description
Food production engineers oversee the electrical and mechanical needs of the equipment
and machinery required in the process of manufacturing food or beverages. They strive to
maximise plant productivity by engaging in preventive actions in reference to health and
safety, good manufacturing practices (GMP), hygiene compliance, and performance of
routine maintenance of machines and equipment.
