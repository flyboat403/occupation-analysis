Description
Concrete finishers work with binding agents like cement and concrete. They put up any
removable forms and pour concrete into the forms. They then execute one or several
actions to finish the concrete: cutting, screeding or levelling, compacting, smoothing, and
chamfering to prevent chipping.
Scope note
Includes people perfomring renovation of concrete and cement. Excludes people
performing insulated concrete forms.
