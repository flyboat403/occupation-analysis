Description
Social care workers provide support and help people with care services. They help people
to live full and valued lives in the community. They assist babies, young children,
adolescents, adults and older adults.They attend to the psychological, social, emotional and
physical needs of service users. They work in a large variety of settings with individuals,
families, groups, organisations and communities.
