Description
Business analysts research and understand the strategic position of businesses and
companies in relation to their markets and their stakeholders. They analyse and present
their views on how the company, from many perspectives, can improve its strategic
position and internal corporate structure. They assess needs for change, communication
methods, technology, IT tools, new standards and certifications.
