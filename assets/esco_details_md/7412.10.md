Description
Power tool repair technicians identify defects and repair electrically powered tools such as
drills, chainsaws, lawn mowers and grinding machines. They disassemble, test and replace
defective parts in tools. Power tools repair technicians advise customers on maintenance
products and methods for their equipment. They may sell such products.
