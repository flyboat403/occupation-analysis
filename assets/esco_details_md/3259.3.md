Description
Chiropractic assistants perform routine and administrative tasks to support patient care
and work under the sole direction and supervision of a Chiropractor or Specialized
Chiropractor. They may interview patients, keep records of the case history, assist
Chiropractors or Specialized Chiropractors in providing patients examination and perform
administrative tasks. Chiropractic assistants work within defined contexts using agreed
protocols and procedures.
