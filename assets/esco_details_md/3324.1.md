Description
Commodity brokers act as intermediary between buyers and sellers of movable and
immovable property such as raw materials, livestock or real estate. They negotiate prices
and receive a commission from the transactions. They research market conditions for
specific commodities in order to inform their clients. They make bid offers and calculate
the cost of transactions.
