Description
Tax advisors use their expertise in tax legislation to provide commercially-focused advisory
and consultancy services to a wide range of clients from all economic sectors. They explain
complicated tax-related legislation to their clients and assist them in ensuring the most
efficient and beneficial payment of taxes by devising tax-efficient strategies. They also
inform them of fiscal changes and developments and may specialise in tax strategies
concerning mergers or multinational reconstruction for business clients, trust and estate
taxes for individual clients etc.
