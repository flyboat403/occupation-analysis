Description
Botanical technicians provide technical assistance in researching and testing different
plant species to monitor their properties such as growth and structure. They collect and
analyse data using laboratory equipment, compile reports and maintain laboratory stock.
Botanical technicians also study plants to research their use in areas such as medicine,
food and materials.
