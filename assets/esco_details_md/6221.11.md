Description
Water-based aquaculture technicians coordinate and supervise activities on fattening
aquaculture organisms in suspended systems (floating or submerged structures). They
participate in extraction operations and handling organisms for commercialisation. Water-
based aquaculture technicians supervise maintenance of equipment and facilities (cages,
rafts, longlines, bouchot).
