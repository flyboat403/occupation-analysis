Description
Emergency ambulance drivers use emergency vehicles to respond to medical emergencies
and support the work of paramedics, move patients safely, take note of changes in the
patient's vital signs and report to the paramedics in charge, ensuring the medical
equipment is well stored, transported and functional, under supervision and on order of a
doctor of medicine.
