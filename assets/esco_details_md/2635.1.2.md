Description
Drug and alcohol addiction counsellors provide assistance and counselling to individuals
and families dealing with drug and alcohol addictions, monitoring their progress,
advocating for them, performing crisis interventions and group therapy. They also assist
the persons with the consequences of their addictions which can be unemployment,
physical or mental disorders and poverty. Drug and alcohol addiction counsellors may also
prepare educational programs for high-risk populations.
