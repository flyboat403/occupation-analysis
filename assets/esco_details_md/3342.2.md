Description
Legal administrative assistants carry out the daily administrative activities of firms, offices
of notaries, and companies. They perform activities such as writing mails, phone-answering
and typing/keyboarding. They combine these activities with specific knowledge and
understanding of the procedures and codes managed in legal business affairs.
