Description
Performance lighting technicians setup, prepare, check and maintain equipment in order to
provide optimal lighting quality for live performances. They cooperate with road crew to
unload, set up and operate lighting equipment and instruments.
Scope note
Excludes people performing media or broadcast activities. Includes people working in
event and rental companies.
