Description
Building cleaners maintain the cleanliness and overall functionality of various types of
buildings such as offices, hospitals and public institutions. They perform cleaning duties
like sweeping, vacuuming and mopping floors, empty trash and check security systems,
locks and windows. Building cleaners check air conditioning systems and notify the
appropriate persons in case of malfunctions or problems.
