Description
Fundraising assistants provide administrative support for fundraising managers. They
target potential donators or sponsors by applying surveys. They process donations and
grant payments, maintain electronic and paper filing systems for all grants and donations,
acknowledge donations and write thank you letters and maintain fundraising records.
