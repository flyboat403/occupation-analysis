Description
Hair stylists wash, dry, cut and style the hair of singers and presenters and various types of
actors, including stage, movie, tv and music video actors. They work together with the art
director to design the look of every person. Hair stylists also dress wigs and hairpieces.
They standby during these artistic activities to touch up the actors' hair or wigs.
Scope note
Excludes hairdressers.
