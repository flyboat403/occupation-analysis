Description
Educational counsellors provide practical and emotional support to students in a
educational institution, either in small groups, classrooms, or individually. They function as
an accessible school official whom students may contact for a wide variety of issues.
Educational counsellors may provide advice on personal problems such as social
integration and behavioural issues, and on school-related matters such as composing
adequate curriculum schedules, discussing test scores, and informing students on further
education options. They may work closely with a school social worker and/or school
psychologist and make referrals to other support services if necessary.
