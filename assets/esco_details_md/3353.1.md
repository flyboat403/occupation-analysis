Description
Social security inspectors investigate fraudulent activities in social security that affect
workers' rights. They audit and examine applications for benefits and investigate company
actions based on employee complaints. Inspections include labour-related activities such as
non-payment of wages or expenses. Social security inspectors ensure that employees are
treated fairly and in accordance to laws. They record and make reports on their findings to
ensure validity of the claims they are investigating.
