Description
Metal annealers operate electric or gas kilns to soften metal so it can be cut and shaped
more easily. They heat the metal to a specific temperature and/or colour and then slowly
cool it, both according to specifications. Metal annealers inspect the metals through the
entire process to observe any flaws.
