Description
Milling machine operators set up, program and control milling machines, designed to cut
excess material from metal workpieces using a computer-controlled rotary-cutting, milling
cutter. They read milling machine blueprints and tooling instructions, perform regular
machine maintenance, and make adjustments to the milling controls, such as the depth of
cuts or the rotation speed.
