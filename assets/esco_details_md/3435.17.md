Description
Prop makers construct, build, prepare, adapt and maintain props used on stage and for
filming movies or television programs. Props may be simple imitations of real life objects,
or may include electronic, pyrotechnical, or other effects. Their work is based on artistic
vision, sketches and plans. They work in close cooperation with the designers involved in
the production.
