Description
Logistics analysts streamline product manufacturing, transportation, storage and
distribution. They assess production and supply chain problems to determine economically
efficient solutions. They assists company managers in decision-making processes and
direct programs designed to provide subcontractors, managers and customers with logistic
technology.
