Description
Aircraft assembly supervisors coordinate the employees involved in aircraft manufacturing
and schedule their activities. They prepare production reports and recommend measures to
reduce the cost and improve productivity such as hiring, ordering new equipment and
implementing new production methods. They train employees in company policies, job
duties and safety measures. They oversee the supplies and communicate with other
departments to avoid unnecessary interruptions of the production process.
