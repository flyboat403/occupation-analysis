Description
Marketing consultants advise companies in the development of marketing strategies for
specific purposes. They can advise and develop strategies for the entry of a brand in the
market, for the re-launch of a product, for the introduction of a new product, or for the
positioning of a commercial image. They perform previous studies of the position of the
company and perception of customers in order to define the marketing approach.
