Description
Train drivers operate trains providing passenger or cargo transport services. They are
responsible for driving the locomotive in safe manner, respecting all relevant safety,
operational and communication regulations, and have full responsibility for the safety of
passengers and cargo. The task involves cooperation and communication with the relevant
staff under the infrastructure manager, as well as with staff on board the train.
