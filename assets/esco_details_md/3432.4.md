Description
Set builders construct, build, prepare, adapt and maintain scenic elements used on stage
and for filming movies or television programs. They use a wide variety of materials like
wood, steel, aluminium and plastics. Their work is based on artistic vision, scale models,
sketches and plans. They work in close cooperation with the designers and may build
exibition stands for fairs, carnavals and other events.
