Description
Braziers operate various equipment and machinery in order to join two metal pieces
together, by heating, melting and forming a metal filler between them, often brass or
copper. They follow a similar process to soldering but with higher temperatures using
torches, soldering irons, fluxes and welding machines to join aluminum, silver, copper, gold
or nickel.
