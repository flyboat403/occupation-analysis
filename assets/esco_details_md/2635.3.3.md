Description
Clinical social workers provide therapy, counselling, and intervention services to clients.
They treat clients with personal struggles, namely mental illness, addiction, and abuse,
advocating for them and helping them gain access to the necessary resources. They also
focus on the impact of medical and public health issues within social aspects.
