Description
Industrial cooks create new food designs and recipes. They prepare, measure and mix
ingredients to prepare foodstuff products. They control and regulate temperatures, monitor
cooking process, assign specific baking tasks, and direct workers in task performance.
Scope note
Excludes cook. Includes industrial chef.
