Description
Policy managers are responsible for managing the development of policy programs and
ensuring that the strategic objectives of the organization are met. They oversee the
production of policy positions, as well as the organization's campaign and advocacy work in
fields such as environmental, ethics, quality, transparency, and sustainability.
