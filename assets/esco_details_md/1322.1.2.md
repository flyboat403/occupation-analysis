Description
Oil and gas production managers coordinate and implement short and medium term oil and
gas production schedules and plans, such as drilling, extraction operations and waste
management. They steer one or more units and oversee the implementation of technical
and human means, within the framework of objectives of volume, quality and planning. Oil
and gas production managers design and ensure that the production plans and schedules
are met.
