Description
Recycling specialists research recycling policies and legislation, and supervise
implementation in an organisation to ensure that waste management occurs according to
regulations. They perform inspections, provide recycling equipment, and supervise
recycling workers. They also advise organisations on ways they can improve their waste
management procedures.
