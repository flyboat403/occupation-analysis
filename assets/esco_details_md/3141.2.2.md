Description
Biochemistry technicians provide technical assistance in researching, analysing and testing
the reactions caused by chemicals in living organisms. They use laboratory equipment to
help develop or improve chemical-based products and also collect and analyse data for
experiments, compile reports and maintain laboratory stock.
