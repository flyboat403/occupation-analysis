Description
Vessel assembly inspectors use measuring and testing equipment to inspect and monitor
boat and ship assemblies to ensure conformity to engineering specifications and to safety
standards and regulations. They examine the assemblies to detect malfunction and damage
and check repair work. They also provide detailed inspection documentation and
recommend action where problems were discovered.
