Description
Financial planners assist people dealing with various personal financial issues. They are
specialised in financial planning, such as retirement planning, investment planning, risk
management and insurance planning, and tax planning. They advise a strategy tailored to
the client's needs. They ensure the accuracy of bank and other financial records while
maintaining a customer-orientated approach and following ethical standards.
