Description
Classical languages teachers at secondary schools provide education to students,
commonly children and young adults, in a secondary school setting. They are usually
subject teachers, specialised and instructing in their own field of study, classical
languages. They prepare lesson plans and materials, monitor the students' progress, assist
individually when necessary, and evaluate the students' knowledge and performance on the
subject of classical languages through assignments, tests and examinations.
