Description
Road construction workers perform road construction on earthworks, substructure works
and the pavement section of the road. They cover the compacted soil with one or more
layers. Road construction workers usually lay a stabilising bed of sand or clay first before
adding asphalt or concrete slabs in order to finish a road.
Scope note
Includes people working with road roller operators. Excludes people performing activities
which involve pouring concrete to serve as a road surface.
