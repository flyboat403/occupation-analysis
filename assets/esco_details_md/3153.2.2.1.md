Description
Airline transport pilots fly large aircrafts with a maximum take-off weight of more than
5700 kilograms, to transport passengers, mail, or freight on long or short-haul flights for
leisure, business or commercial purposes. They have the overall responsibility for the safe
and efficient operation of aircrafts and the safety of crew and passengers.
