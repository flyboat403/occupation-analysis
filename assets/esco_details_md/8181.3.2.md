Description
Tunnel kiln operators control preheating chambers and tunnel kilns to preheat and bake
clay products, such as bricks, sewer popes, mosaic, ceramic or quarry tiles. They observe
gauges and instruments and adjust by turning valves if necessary. They pull loaded kiln
cars in and out of the heaters and move them to a sorting area.
