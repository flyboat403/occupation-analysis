Description
Computer-aided design operators use computer hardware and software in order to add the
technical dimensions to computer aided design drawings. Computer-aided design operators
ensure all additional aspects of the created images of products are accurate and realistic.
They also calculate the amount of materials needed to manufacture the products. Later the
finalised digital design is processed by computer-aided manufacturing machines that
produce the finished product.
