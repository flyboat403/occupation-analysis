Description
Business managers are responsible for setting the objectives of the business unit of a
company, creating a plan for the operations, and facilitating the achievement of the
objectives and implementation of the plan together with employees of the segment and
stakeholders. They keep an overview of the business, understand detailed information of
the business unit and support the department, and make decisions based on the
information at hand.
