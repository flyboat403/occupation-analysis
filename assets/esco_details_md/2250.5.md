Description
Aquatic animal health professionals diagnose, prevent and treat diseases, injuries and
dysfunctions of aquatic animals by implementing appropriate sampling protocols. They
supervise the use of medicines, including vaccines, and collect data on fish health, making
regular reports to the appropriate personnel. They may provide care to a wide range of
aquatic animals or specialise in the treatment of a particular group or in a particular
speciality area. They may provide advice, support and training to farm staff on best
practice with regard to the health and welfare of the cultured organisms.
