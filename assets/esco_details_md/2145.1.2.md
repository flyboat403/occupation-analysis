Description
Brewmasters ensure brewing quality of current products and create mixtures for the
development of new products. For current products, they oversee the whole brewing
process following one of many brewing processes. For new products, they develop new
brewing formulas and processing techniques or modify existing ones as to come up with
potential new products.
