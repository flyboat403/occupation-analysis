Description
Manufacturing facility managers foresee the maintenance and routine operational planning
of buildings intended to be used for manufacturing activities. They control and manage
health and safety procedures, supervise the work of contractors, plan and handle buildings
maintenance operations, fire safety and security issues, and oversee buildings' cleaning
activities.
