Description
eBusiness managers create and execute a company's electronic strategy plan for selling
products and services online. They also improve data integrity, placement of online tools
and brand exposure and monitor sales for companies that market products to customers
using the internet. They collaborate with the marketing and sales management team using
ICT tools to reach sales goals and provide accurate information and offerings to business
partners.
