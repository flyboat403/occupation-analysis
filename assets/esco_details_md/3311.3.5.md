Description
Stock traders use their technical expertise of financial markets performance to advise and
make recommendations to asset managers or shareholders for a profitable investment
strategy, keeping in mind the company’s performance. They use stock market trading
operations and deal with a wide array of taxes, commissions and fiscal obligations. Stock
traders buy and sell bonds, stocks, futures and shares in hedge funds. They perform
detailed micro- and macroeconomic and industry specific technical analysis.
