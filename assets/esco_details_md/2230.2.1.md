Description
Acupuncturists make use of thin needles and insert them into acupuncture points in the
body in order to restore the imbalances of the human body and overall good health.
Acupuncturists focus on energy meridians to treat various conditions.
Scope note
Includes doctors of medicine or other healthcare professionals who perform acupuncture.
