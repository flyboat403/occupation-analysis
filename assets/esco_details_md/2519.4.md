Description
ICT disaster recovery analysts develop, maintain, and implement ICT continuity and
disaster recovery strategies and solutions. They support the technical teams, assess the
risks, design and develop procedures, documentation and strategies for disaster recovery
in order for business functions to continue and recover with a minimal loss of data. They
also coordinate the system backups tests and validations.
Scope note
Excludes people performing development activities.
