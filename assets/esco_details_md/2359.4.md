Description
Assessors of prior learning measure the existing competences, skills and knowledge of a
candidate against a given qualification or certificate, according to a pre-defined standard
or procedure, and judge objectively if the candidate reaches the corresponding
performance criteria.
Scope note
The assessors of prior learning have a substantial demonstrable experience in the targeted
occupations they are assessing.
