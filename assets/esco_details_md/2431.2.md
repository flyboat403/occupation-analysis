2431.2
Description
Advertising media buyers purchase, on behalf of their clients, advertising space in the
print, broadcast and online media. They analyse the effectiveness and appropriateness of
the different channels depending on the good or service, providing advice for the decision-
making. They try to negotiate the best price, without compromising the quality of the
advertisements. They support the development and implementation of marketing and
advertising plans through the most suited media outlet.
Scope note
Includes people working with the advertising media planner.
