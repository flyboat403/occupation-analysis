Description
Market research analysts collect the information gathered in the market research and
study it to draw conclusions. They define the potential customers of a product, the target
group and the way they can be reached. Market research analysts analyse the position of
products in the market from different perspectives such as features, prices and
competitors. They analyse cross selling and interdependencies between different products
and their placement. Market research analysts prepare information helpful for the
development of marketing strategies.
