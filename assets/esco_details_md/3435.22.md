Description
Stage managers coordinate and supervise the preparation and execution of the show to
ensure the scenic image and the actions on stage comply with the artistic vision of the
director and the artistic team.
They identify needs, monitor the technical and artistic processes during rehearsals and
performances of live shows and events, according to the artistic project, the characteristics
of the stage and technical, economic, human and security terms.
