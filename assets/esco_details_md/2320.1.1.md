Description
Agriculture, forestry and fishery vocational teachers instruct students in their specialised
field of study, agriculture, forestry and fishery, which is predominantly practical in nature.
They provide theoretical instruction in service of the practical skills and techniques the
students must subsequently master for an agriculture, forestry or fishery profession.
Agriculture, forestry and fishery vocational teachers monitor the students' progress, assist
individually when necessary, and evaluate their knowledge and performance on the subject
of agriculture, forestry and fishery through assignments, tests and examinations.
