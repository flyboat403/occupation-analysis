Description
Investment managers administer the portfolio of investments that a company has. They
perform close follow up of the investments looking for the most profitable solutions
represented in financial products or securities. They analyse behaviour in financial
markets, interests rates, and the companies' position in order to advise on risks and
profitability for the client.
Scope note
Includes people performing financial planning, strategy and research.
