Description
Corrosion technicians monitor the integrity from a pipeline and provide repairs to it if
needed. They ensure that the pipelines are properly connected and are in line with the
health and safety regulations. Corrosion technicians inspect cathodic protection systems
and pipeline connection points for corrosion. They may also assist in the designing of
pipelines, analyse soil and write reports on technical issues.
