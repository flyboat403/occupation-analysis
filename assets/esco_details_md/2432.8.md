Description
Public affairs consultants function as representatives for a client's goal. They attempt to
persuade legislative bodies and policy makers to implement laws or regulation in
accordance with the client's wishes, and negotiate with parties with potentially conflicting
interests. They perform analytical and research duties in order to ensure that the client's
cause is being addressed in an appropriate way to the appropriate parties. They also
consult their clients on their causes and policies.
