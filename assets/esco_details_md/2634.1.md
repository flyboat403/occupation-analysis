Description
Polygraph examiners prepare individuals for polygraph testing, conduct the polygraph
exam and interpret the results. They pay close attention to detail and use a range of
instruments to monitor respiratory, sweat and cardiovascular responses to questions
addressed during the process. Polygraph examiners write reports on the basis of the
results and can provide courtroom testimony.
