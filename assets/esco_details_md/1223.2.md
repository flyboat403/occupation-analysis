Description
Research and development managers coordinate the efforts of scientists, academical
researchers, product developers, and market researchers towards the creation of new
products, the improvement of current ones or other research activities, including scientific
research. They manage and plan research and development activities of an organisation,
specify goals and budget requirements and manage the staff.
Scope note
Includes people performing academical research work, product development research and
business research and development.
