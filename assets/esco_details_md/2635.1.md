Description
Social counsellors provide support and guidance to individuals in the social work area, to
help them solve specific problems in their personal life. It involves addressing personal and
relationship issues, dealing with inner conflicts, crisis moments such as depression and
addiction, in an attempt to empower individuals to achieve change and improve their life
quality.
Scope note
Excludes people performing counselling services in the medical therapeutic sense.
