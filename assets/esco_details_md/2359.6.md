Description
E-learning architects establish goals and procedures for the application of learning
technologies within an organisation and the creation of an infrastructure that supports
these goals and procedures. They review the existing curriculum of courses and verify the
online delivery capability, advising changes to the curriculum to adapt to online delivery.
