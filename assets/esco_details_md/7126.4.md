Description
Heating technicians install and maintain gas, electric, oil, solid-fuel and multiple fuel
heating and ventilation equipment as stand-alone heating and ventilation systems or build
into machinery and transport equipment. They follow instructions and blueprints, perform
maintenance on systems, perform safety checks and repair the systems.
