Description
Landscape architects plan and design the construction of gardens and natural spaces. They
determine the specifications and distribution of the space. Moreover, landscape architects
research and analyse the potential of the landscape at all stages, scales and contexts of the
development process, combining, an understanding of the natural space with a sense of
aesthetics in order to create a harmonious space.
