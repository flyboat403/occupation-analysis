Description
Scientific laboratory technicians carry out laboratory-based research, analysis and testing
and support life science professionals. They sample, test, measure, research and analyse in
areas such as biology, biotechnology, environmental science, forensic science and
pharmacology. Scientific laboratory technicians also observe and monitor laboratory
activities, record test sequences and analyse the results.
Scope note
Excludes medical laboratory assistant.
