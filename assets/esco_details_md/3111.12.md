Description
Radiation protection technicians monitor radiation levels in buildings and facilities to
ensure compliance with health and safety standards, and to prevent dangerous elevations
in the radiation level. They take measures to minimise radiation emissions, and to prevent
further contamination in the event of radiation pollution, by developing radiation
protection plans, in particular for nuclear plants and facilities.
