Description
Digital literacy teachers instruct students in the theory and practice of (basic) computer
usage. They teach students digital literacy and, optionally, more advanced principles of
computer science. They prepare the students with knowledge of software programmes
ensure that computer hardware equipment is properly used. Digital literacy teachers
construct and revise course content and assignments, and update them according to
technological developments.
