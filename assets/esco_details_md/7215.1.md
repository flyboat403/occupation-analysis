Description
Event scaffolders set up and dismantle temporary seating, stages and structures which
support performance equipment, artists and the audience. Their job can include rope
access, working above colleagues and lifting heavy loads, which makes it a high risk
occupation. Their work is based on instruction, plans and calculations. They work indoors
as well as outdoors.
Scope note
Includes people working in event and rental companies. Excludes people performing
industrial or construction scaffolding.
