Description
Snowboard instructors teach groups or individuals how to ride a snowboard. They instruct
students of all ages and skill level individually or in groups. Snowboard instructors teach
basic and advanced techniques of snowboarding by demonstrating exercises and giving
feedback to students. They give advice on safety and on snowboarding equipment.
