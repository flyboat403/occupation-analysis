Description
Police officers use investigation methods to prevent crime, to pursue and apprehend
criminals, and protect the public from violent and criminal activities. They perform
surveillance and patrol activities to prevent crimes and apprehend offenders. They provide
support to victimised parties and the public at large, and fulfil administrative duties.
Scope note
Includes state police officer.
