Description
Onshore wind farm technicians operate and maintain onshore wind farms by performing
diagnostic inspections, analysing faults, and performing repair duties. They ensure the
wind turbines operate in compliance with regulations and assist the wind engineers in the
construction of wind turbines. Onshore wind farm technicians may also test and install
hardware and software components of wind turbines.
