Description
Customer experience managers monitor customer experiences by creating, evaluating and
improving the customer's interaction organisations in the hospitality, recreation or
entertainment industry. They develop plans of action to optimise all aspects of the
customer's experience. Customer experience managers strive to ensure customer
satisfaction and boost company profits.
