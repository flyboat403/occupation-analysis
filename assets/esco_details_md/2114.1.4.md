Description
Hydrogeologists in mining study the distribution, quality and flow of water in order to keep
mine workings free of nuisance water and to ensure an adequate supply of process water.
They supply and evaluate information which will protect ground and surface water from
contamination.
Scope note
Excludes hydrologists.
