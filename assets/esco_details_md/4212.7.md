Description
Odds compilers are in charge of counting the odds in gambling. They are employed by a
bookmaker, betting exchange, lotteries and digital/on-line as well as casinos who set the
odds for events (such as sporting outcomes) for customers to place bets on. Apart from
pricing markets, they also engage in any activity regarding the trading aspects of
gambling, such as monitoring customer accounts and the profitability of their operations.
Odds compilers may be required to monitor the financial position the bookmaker is in and
adjust their position (and odds) accordingly. They may also be consulted as to whether to
accept a bet or not.
