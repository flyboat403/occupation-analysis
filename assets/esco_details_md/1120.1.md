Description
Animal facility managers coordinate and plan all activities of a zoo. They formulate policies,
manage daily operations, and plan the use of materials and human resources. They are the
driving force and public face of their institution. This often involves representing their
institution on a national, regional and global scale and taking part in coordinated zoo
activities.
Scope note
Excludes people working in animal research facilities.
