Description
Metal additive manufacturing operators operate machines using additive manufacturing
processes, such as fitting and setting up, maintenance and repair. They have factual and
broad understanding in the field of metal additive manufacturing process. They are able to
develop solutions on basic and specific problems related with additive manufacturing
machines and processes and self-manage the handling of feedstock (approval, storage,
contamination, traceability).
