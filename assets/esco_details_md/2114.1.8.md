Description
Oceanographers study and perform research on matters related to the sea and oceans.
Oceanographers divide their expertise in different branches of research which are physical
oceanographers whose research focus on waves and tides, chemical oceanographers whose
study address the chemical constitution of sea waters, and geological oceanographer
whose research refers to the bottom of seas and their plaques.
