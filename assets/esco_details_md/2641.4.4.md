Description
Technical communicators prepare clear, concise and professional communication from
product developers to users of the products such as online help, user manuals, white
papers, specifications and industrial videos. For this, they analyse products, legal
requirements, markets, customers and users. They develop information and media
concepts, standards, structures and software tool support. They plan the content creation
and media production processes, develop written, graphical, video or other contents,
generate media output, release their information products and receive feedback from the
users.
