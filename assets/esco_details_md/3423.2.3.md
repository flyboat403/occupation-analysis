Description
Pilates teachers plan, teach, and adapt exercises based on the work and principles of
Joseph Pilates. They collect and analyse information for each client to ensure that
programmes are safe, appropriate and effective. They apply the principles of Pilates
through the planning and teaching of supportive, non-competitive lesson. They motivate
and encourage clients to ensure their adherence to regular sessions.
