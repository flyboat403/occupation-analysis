Description
Project support officers provide different types of services for the successful execution of a
project as part of a horizontal project management office. They offer administrative
support, assistance and training to project managers and other staff members, manage the
project’s documentation and assist the project manager with project scheduling, resource
planning, coordination and reporting. Project support officers are responsible for quality
assurance activities and for monitoring the adherence to methodology guidelines and other
organisational standards. They also offer advice on project management tools and related
administrative services.
