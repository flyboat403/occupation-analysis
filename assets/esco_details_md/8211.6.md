Description
Rolling stock assemblers use hand tools, power tools and other equipment such as lifting
equipment or robots to construct, fit and install prefabricated parts to manufacture rolling
stock subassemblies and body structures. They read and interpret blueprints. They operate
control systems to determine functional performance of the assemblies and adjust
accordingly.
