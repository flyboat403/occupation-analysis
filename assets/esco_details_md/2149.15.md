Description
Robotics engineers design and develop robotic devices and applications in combination
with mechanical engineering principles. They use pre-established designs and current
developments for improving or inventing systems, machinery and equipment. They
combine several knowledge fields such as computing, engineering, and electronics in the
development of new engineering applications.
