Description
Missionaries supervise the execution of missions of outreach from a church foundation.
They organise the mission and develop the mission's goals and strategies, and ensure the
mission's goals are executed, and policies implemented. They perform administrative
duties for record maintenance, and facilitate communication with the relevant institutions
in the mission's location.
