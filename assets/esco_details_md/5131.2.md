Description
Waiters/waitresses supply guests with food and drinks as requested. Waiters/waitresses
usually work in restaurants, bars and hotels. This involves the preparation of tables,
serving food or beverages and taking payments.
Scope note
Excludes head waiter/head waitress. Excludes bartender.
