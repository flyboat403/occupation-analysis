Description
Vloggers make online videos to talk about a wide range of subjects such as politics,
fashion, economics and sports. They can relate objective facts, but often they also give
their opinion on the related topic. Vloggers post these videos online on social media or
streaming platforms, often accompanied with written text. They also interact with their
viewers via comments.
