Description
Physicists are scientists who study physical phenomena. They focus their research
depending on their specialisation, which can range from atomic particle physics to the
study of phenomena in the universe. They apply their findings for the improvement of
society by contributing to the development of energy supplies, treatment of illness, game
development, cutting-edge equipment, and daily use objects.
Scope note
Includes people performing specialised activities within nuclear, molecular, optical physics,
astrophysics, medical physics, economical physics and matter physics.
