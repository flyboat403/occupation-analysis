Description
Head waiters/waitresses manage the food and beverage service in a hospitality outlet or
unit. They are responsible for the customer's experience. Head waiters/waitresses
coordinate all actions involving customers such as welcoming guests, ordering, delivering
the food and beverages and supervise financial transactions.
