Description
Animal shelter workers provide animal care routine services at the animal shelter. They
receive animals brought to the shelter, respond to calls about lost or injured animals, nurse
animals, clean cages, handle papers for adoption of animals, transport animals to the
veterinarian and maintain database with the animals present in the shelter.
