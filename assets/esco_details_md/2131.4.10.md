Description
Microbiologists study and research the life forms, characteristics, and processes of
microscopic organisms. They study microorganisms such as bacteria, protozoa, fungi, etc.
in order to diagnose and counteract the effects that these microorganisms might have in
animals, in the environment, in the food industry, or in the health care industry.
