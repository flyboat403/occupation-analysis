Description
Prosthetic-orthotics technicians design, create, fit and repair supportive devices, such as
braces, joints, arch supports, and other surgical and medical appliances.
Scope note
Includes people producing orthopaedic devices. Excludes people producing electrical
devices such as hearing aids.
