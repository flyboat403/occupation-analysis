Description
3D Printing technicians assist in the designing and programming of products, ranging from
prosthetic products to 3D miniatures. They may also provide 3D printing maintenance,
check 3D renders for customers and run 3D printing tests. 3D printing technicians can also
repair, maintain and clean 3D printers.
