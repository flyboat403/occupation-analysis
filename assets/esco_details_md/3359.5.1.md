Description
Marine cargo inspectors investigate all baggage and freight listed on the ship's manifest.
They are responsible for checking the condition of the cargo and verifying the cargo's
documentation and compliance with rules and regulations. Marine cargo inspectors
analyse the vessels' capabilities, the fuel compartments, the safety equipment and the
specific licenses required.
