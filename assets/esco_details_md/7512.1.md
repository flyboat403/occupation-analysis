Description
Bakers make a wide range of breads, pastries, and other baked goods. They follow all the
processes from receipt and storage of raw materials, preparation of raw materials for
bread-making, measurement and mixing of ingredients into dough and proof. They tend
ovens to bake products to an adequate temperature and time.
