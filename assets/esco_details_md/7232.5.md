Description
Aircraft maintenance technicians perform preventive maintenance to aircrafts, aircraft
components, engines and assemblies, such as airframes and hydraulic and pneumatic
systems. They perform inspections following strict protocols and aviation laws.
Scope note
Excludes people performing maintenance and repair of navigation and communication
systems.
