Description
Lime kiln operators control equipment that burns limestone to produce lime or carbon
dioxide. They control the flow of the materials, fuel, and air and observe the material flow,
air, temperature, fuel charts, and oxygen gauges and make adjustments if necessary. They
also keep production records.
