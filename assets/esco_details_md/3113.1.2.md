Description
Electromechanical engineering technicians collaborate with electromechanical engineers
in the development of electromechanical equipment. Electromechanical engineering
technicians are responsible for building, installing, testing, monitoring, and maintaining
the electromechanical equipment, circuits and systems. They test this by the use of test
instruments such as oscilloscopes and voltmeters. Electromechanical engineering
technicians also use soldering equipment and hand tools to repair electromechanical
equipment.
