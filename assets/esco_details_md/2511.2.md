Description
Computer vision engineers research, design, develop, and train artificial intelligence
algorithms and machine learning primitives that understand the content of digital images
based on a large amount of data. They apply this understanding to solve different real-
world problems such as security, autonomous driving, robotic manufacturing, digital image
classification, medical image processing and diagnosis, etc.
