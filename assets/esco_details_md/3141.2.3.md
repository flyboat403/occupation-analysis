Description
Biology technicians provide technical assistance in researching and analysing the
relationship between living organisms and their environment. They use laboratory
equipment to examine organic substances such as bodily fluids, medicines, plants and food.
They collect and analyse data for experiments, compile reports and maintain laboratory
stock.
