Description
ICT system testers perform testing activities and some test planning activities. They may
also debug and repair ICT systems and components although this mainly corresponds to
designers and developers. They ensure that all systems and components function properly
before delivering them to internal and external clients.
Scope note
Excludes people performing managerial activities.
