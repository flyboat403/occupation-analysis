Description
Animal handlers are in charge of handling animals in a working role and continue the
training of the animal, in accordance with national legislation.
Scope note
Includes people working with security and military dogs, search and rescue dogs,
laboratory animals, assistance dogs or show animals. Includes stud animal handlers.
