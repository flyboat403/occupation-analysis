Description
Flight test engineers work with other systems engineers to plan the tests in detail and to
make sure that the recording systems are installed for the required data parameters. They
analyse the data collected during test flights and produce reports for individual test phases
and for the final flight test. They are also responsible for the safety of the test operations.
