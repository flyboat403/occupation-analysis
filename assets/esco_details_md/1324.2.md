Description
Air traffic managers coordinate the planning of ground control, the maintenance of the
aircrafts, and the handling of customers. They strive for the most efficient use of resources
in directing the aircraft. They manage safety, quality, and risks in everyday work. They also
plan and compare performance with other air navigation service providers.
