Description
Sewer construction workers install sewer pipes to transport wastewater out of structures
and to a body of water or treatment facility. They dig trenches and insert the pipes, making
sure they have correct angle and are connected watertigh. Sewer construction workers
also construct other elements of sewage infrastructure, such as manholes, and maintain
and repair existing systems.
