Description
Hotel concierges provide information to customers, assist guests with various tasks such as
making restaurant reservations, recommending entertainment highlights, booking
transportation (limousines, airplanes, boats, etc.) and other services, procurement of
tickets to special events, and assisting with various travel arrangements and tours of local
attractions.
