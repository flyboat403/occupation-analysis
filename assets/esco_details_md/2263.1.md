Description
Emergency response coordinators analyse potential risks such as disasters and
emergencies for a community or institution and develop a strategy for reacting to these
risks. They outline guidelines for the response to an emergency in order to decrease the
effects. They educate the parties at risk on these guidelines. They also test response plans
and ensure that the necessary supplies and equipments are in place in compliance with
health and safety regulations.
