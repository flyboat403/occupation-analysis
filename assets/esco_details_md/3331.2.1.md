Description
Import export specialists have and apply deep knowledge of import and export goods
including customs clearance and documentation. They declare goods that cross the border,
inform customers about customs and give advice concerning disputes related to customs
legislation. They prepare the documents needed and make sure they are delivered to
customs. They check and process duty and make sure VAT payments are done as
applicable.
