Description
Clothing CAD patternmakers design, evaluate, adjust and modify patterns, cutting plans
and technical files for all kinds of wearing apparel using CAD systems, acting as interfaces
with digital printing, cutting and assembly operations, being aware of the technical
requirements on quality, manufacturability and cost assessment.
