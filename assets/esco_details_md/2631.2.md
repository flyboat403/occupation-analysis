Description
Economists perform research and develop theories in the field of economics, whether for
microeconomic or macroeconomic analysis. They study trends, analyse statistical data, and
to some extent work with economic mathematical models in order to advise companies,
governments, and related institutions. They advise on product feasibility, trend forecasts,
emerging markets, tax policies, and consumer trends.
