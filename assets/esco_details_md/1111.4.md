Description
Mayors chair their jurisdiction's council meetings and act as main supervisor of the local
government's administrative and operational policies. They also represent their jurisdiction
in ceremonial and official events and promote activities and events. They, together with the
council, hold the local or regional legislative power and oversee development and
implementation of policies. They also supervise staff and perform administrative duties.
