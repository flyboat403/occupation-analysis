Description
Podiatry assistants help the podiatrist by carrying out supportive tasks such as assisting in
diagnosing and treating the disorders, diseases and deformities of the feet, advising people
on foot care techniques and the importance of foot care for promoting better mobility and
general well-being. They also perform small tasks such as cutting toe nails, applying
plasters and general clerical duties.
