Description
Shipbrokers act as intermediaries between buyers and sellers of ships, cargospace on ships
and charter ships for the transfer of cargo. They inform clients on the shipping market
mechanisms and movements, report on vessel and cargospace prices and sales, and
negotiate not only the cost of the vessels, cargospace or cargo but also the logistical
requirements for the transfer of the vessel or commodity cargo to the buyers.
