Description
Sports officials are responsible for administering the rules and laws of a sport and for
ensuring fair play according to the rules and laws. The role includes applying rules during
the sport or activity, contributing to health, safety and protection of participants and others
during the sport or activity, organising sport events, establishing and maintaining effective
working relationships with competitors and others, and communicating effectively.
