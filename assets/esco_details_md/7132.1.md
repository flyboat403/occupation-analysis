Description
Lacquer spray gun operators operate lacquer spray guns designed to provide otherwise
finished metal, wooden or plastic workpieces with a hard, durable finishing coat, through
lacquer coating or paint that is either matte, sheen or highly glossy, but is always meant
for hard surfaces.
