Description
Performance artists create a performance which can be any situation that involves four
basic elements: time, space, the performer's body, or presence in a medium, and a
relationship between the performer and audience or onlookers. They are flexible with the
medium of the art work, the setting and the length of time of the performance.
Scope note
Excludes street artist, puppeteer and variety artist.
