Description
Occupational health and safety inspectors perform workplace audits to ensure compliance
with government and environment legislation. They also investigate work accidents.
Occupational health and safety inspectors interview employees to ensure the work
environment is compliant with health and safety rules, inspect the physical work site and
analyse legal paperwork.
