Description
Deputy head teachers support the management duties of their school's principals and are
part of the school's administrative staff. They update the head teacher on the daily
operations and developments of the school. They implement and follow up on school
guidelines, policies and curriculum activities introduced by the specific head teacher. They
enforce school board protocol, supervise students and maintain discipline.
