3435.3
Description
Audio production technicians set up, prepare, check and maintain equipment in order to
provide optimal sound quality for a live performance. They cooperate with road crew to
unload, set up and operate sound equipment and instruments.
Scope note
Excludes people performing media or broadcast activities. Includes people working in
event and rental companies.
