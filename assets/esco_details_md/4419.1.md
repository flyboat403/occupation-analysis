Description
Advertising assistants provide support in all the operations carried out in advertising
departments or advertising agencies. They ensure that administrative activities are handle
and sort out. They communicate with the staff of the advertisement department and with
external clients. Moreover, they have understanding of the operations in advertising as to
assist with the pragmatic implementation of plans and tasks carried out by managers and
consultants and for being involved in creating content for use both on-line or in print.
