Description
Lawyers provide legal advice to clients and act on their behalf in legal proceedings and in
compliance with the law. They research for, interpret and study cases to represent their
clients in a variety of settings such as courts and administrative boards. They create
arguments on behalf of their clients for lawsuits in different contexts with the aim of
finding a legal remedy.
