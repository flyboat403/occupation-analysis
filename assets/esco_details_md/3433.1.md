Description
Art handlers are trained individuals who work directly with objects in museums and art
galleries. They work in coordination with exhibition registrars, collection
managers, conservator-restorers and curators, among others, to ensure that objects are
safely handled and cared for. Often they are responsible for packing and unpacking art,
installing and deinstalling art in exhibitions, and moving art around the museum and
storage spaces.
