Description
Building information modelling consultants advice, guide and evaluate clients in building
information modelling (BIM) business processes, as well as construction teams on
performing BIM technologies. They manage the communication with staff and clients,
implement strategies and BIM solutions for completing the deliverables, and draft
documents such as flow charts, agendas and guides for best practices.
