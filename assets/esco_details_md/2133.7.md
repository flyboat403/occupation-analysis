Description
Environmental scientists identify problems and find solutions in order to minimize
environmental hazards by performing analysis on samples such as air, water or soil. They
advise on or develop environmental policies and aim to improve water supplies
preservation and manage waste disposal sites. Environmental scientists perform
environmental risk assessments and analyse the environmental impact of new solutions,
construction sites or environmental changes ensuring the environmental regulations are
followed.
