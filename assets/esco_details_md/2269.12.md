Description
Anatomical pathology technicians assist specialised doctors in pathology in conducting
post-mortem examinations, keeping records of the samples, specimens, organs and the
respective findings and taking care of their appropriate disposal under supervision,
following the orders of the doctor of medicine.
Scope note
Excludes people performing patients treatment. Excludes people performing assistance in
medical treatment.
