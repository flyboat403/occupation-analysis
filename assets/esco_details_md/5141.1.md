Description
Hairdressers offer beauty services such as cutting, colouring, bleaching, permanent waving
and styling clients' hair. They ask their clients about their hairstyle preferences in order to
provide customised services. Hairdressers use clippers, scissors and razors. They provide
hair and scalp treatments and shampoo, condition and rinse hair.
