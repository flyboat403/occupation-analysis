Code
3435.15.1
Description
Intelligent lighting engineers set up, prepare, check and maintain digital and automated
lighting equipment in order to provide optimal lighting quality for a live performance. They
cooperate with road crew to unload, set up and operate lighting equipment and
instruments.
Scope note
Includes people working in event and rental companies.
