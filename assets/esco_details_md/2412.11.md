Description
Venture capitalists invest in young or small start-up companies by providing private
funding. They research potential markets and particular product opportunities to help
business owners develop or expand a business. They provide business advice, technical
expertise, and network contacts based on their experience and activities. They do not
assume executive managerial positions within the company, but have a say in its strategic
direction.
