Description
Occupational railway instructors teach future occupational drivers of railway
transportation, such as train, tram, metro and trolley drivers how to operate their railway
vehicle according to regulations. They teach their students theory and techniques on how
to optimally drive and maintain their specific railway vehicle, observe and evaluate the
students' practice. They also focus on non-driving related subjects such as customer
service (in case of person transportation) and safety measure regulations.
