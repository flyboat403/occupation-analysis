Description
Promoters work with artists (or their agents) and venues on to arrange a show. They liaise
with bands and agents to agree on a date for a performance and negotiate a deal. They
book a venue and promote the upcoming gig. They make sure everything the band needs is
in place and set up soundcheck times and the running order of the show. Some promoters
work freelance, but they may also be tied to a single venue or festival.
