Description
Intellectual property consultants provide advice on the usage of intellectual property
assests such as patents, copyrights, and trademarks. They help clients to value, in
monetary terms, intellectual property portfolios, to follow adequate legal procedures for
protecting of such property, and to perform patent brokerage activities.
