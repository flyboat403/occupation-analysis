Description
Smart city consultants advise on sustainability solutions for the implementation of smart
city initiatives. They execute environmental projects based on smart services, advanced
technology, and digital transformation in urban areas. In addition, Smart city consultants
develop business plans to attract investment resources, identify challenges and sustainable
business opportunities, as well as synergies between public and private stakeholders or
clients.
