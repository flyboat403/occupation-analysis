Description
Solderers operate various equipment and machinery such as gas torches, soldering irons,
welding machines, or electric-ultrasonic equipment in order to solder together two or more
items (usually metals), by melting and forming a metal filler in between the joints, the filler
metal has a lower melting point than the adjoining metal.
