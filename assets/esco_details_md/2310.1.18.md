Description
Food science lecturers are subject professors, teachers, or lecturers who instruct students
who have obtained an upper secondary education diploma in their own specialised field of
study, food science, which is predominantly academic in nature. They work with their
university research assistants and university teaching assistants for the preparation of
lectures and of exams, grading papers and exams and leading review and feedback
sessions for the students. They also conduct academic research in their field of food
science, publish their findings and liaise with other university colleagues.
