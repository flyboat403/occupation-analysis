Description
Zoo section leaders are responsible for managing and leading a team of zoo keepers. They
will carry out daily care and management of animals as well as, in cooperation with
colleagues, long term management and organisation of the species and exhibits in their
section. They are also accountable for various aspects of staff management for the keepers
in their section. Depending on the size of the zoo and animal section they may have
additional responsibility for appointing staff and budgeting.
