Description
Make-up artists assist and support artists before, during and after the performance and
filming of movies or television programs to ensure the make-up is in line with the artistic
vision of the director and the artistic team. They create images and characters through
make-up and prosthetics. They maintain, check and repair prosthetics and assist with quick
changes.
