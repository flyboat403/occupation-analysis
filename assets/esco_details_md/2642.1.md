Description
Journalists research, verify and write news stories for newspapers, magazines, television
and other broadcast media. They cover political, economic, cultural, social and sport
events. Journalists must conform to ethical codes such as freedom of speech and right of
reply, press law and editorial standards in order to bring objective information.
