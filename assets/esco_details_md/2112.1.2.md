Description
Climatologists study the average change in weather and climate from a long-term
perspective. They research and analyse historic weather conditions in order to forecast
climatic conditions trends such as changes in temperature, global warming, or regional
evolutive weather conditions. They use these findings for advising on environmental policy,
construction, agricultural projects, and societal matters.
