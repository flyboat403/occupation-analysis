Description
Petroleum pump system operators tend pumps that keep the circulation of oil and derived
products running smoothly. They monitor the flow within the pipes at a refinery and test
the equipment to ensure minimum disruptions. Pump systems operators work from a highly
automated control room, where they communicate with other workers to coordinate pump
activities. Pump system operators undertake minor repairs and maintenance, and report as
called for.
