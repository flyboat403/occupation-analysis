Description
Artificial intelligence engineer apply methods of artificial intelligence in engineering,
robotics and computer science to design programs which simulate intelligence including
thinking models, cognitive and knowledge-based systems, problem solving, and decision
making. They also integrate structured knowledge into computer systems (ontologies,
knowledge bases) in order to solve complex problems normally requiring a high level of
human expertise or artificial intelligence methods.
Scope note
Excludes people performing managerial activities.
