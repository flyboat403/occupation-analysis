Description
Marine mechanics are in charge of the engines and mechanical parts of the vessel and
replace defective equipment and parts. They communicate with other crew members on
the operational level. Marine mechanics maintain and repair the engines and other
machinery of vessels such as boilers, generators and electrical equipment.
