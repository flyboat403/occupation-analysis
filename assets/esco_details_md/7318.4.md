Description
Knitters create textiles or fabric by knitting pieces of yarn. They use traditional techniques
to create interlocking loops of yarn which form a uniform piece of fabric. Knitters use
different techniques, needles and yarn materials to create knitted materials of diverse
proportions.
Scope note
Excludes knitting machine supervisor.
