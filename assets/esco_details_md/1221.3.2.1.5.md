Description
Aftersales service managers are responsible for the lifecycle of the products delivered to
customers for at least the warranty period, after which, in accordance with customer
needs, for the sale of spare parts and technical support. They recruit the staff and
collaborators, exercise leadership, and follow KPIs for products stocks, margins and
orders. Ensure that safety rules and procedures are followed by the whole team. Manage
the claims and guarantee-related processes.
