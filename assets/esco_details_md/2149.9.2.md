Description
Energy systems engineers supervise the energy conversion and distribution processes.
They analyse the energy supply and consumption efficiency developing new ways to
improve the existing processes, taking into account both the technical and the financial
aspects. They also study the environmental impact of energy usage and combine the
production of renewable energy in the current power systems.
