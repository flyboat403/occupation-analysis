Description
Further education teachers organise and teach programmes designed specifically for adult
learners. They impart a wide spectrum of subjects, ranging from academic areas such as
mathematics and history, to trainings for personality development, technical expertise or
practical courses like languages and ICT. They teach and support adults aspiring to
broaden their knowledge and their personal and professional skills and/or to achieve
further qualifications. Further education teachers consider the previous knowledge and the
work and life experience of the learners. They individualize their teaching and involve the
students in the planning and executing of their learning activities. Further education
teachers design reasonable assignments and examinations suitable to their adult learners.
