Description
Marine engineering technicians carry out technical functions to help marine engineers
with the design, development, manufacturing and testing processes, installation and
maintenance of all types of boats from pleasure crafts to naval vessels, including
submarines. They also conduct experiments, collect and analyse data and report their
findings.
