Description
Political scientists study political behaviour, activity and systems, including the elements
that fall therein. Their study of the field ranges from the origins and evolution of various
political systems to topical matters such as decision-making processes, political behaviour,
political trends, society, and power perspectives. They advise governments and
institutional organisations on governance matters.
