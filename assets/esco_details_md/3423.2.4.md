Description
Sport therapists program and supervise rehabilitation exercises for individuals and groups.
They work with individuals that have chronic health conditions or are at high risk of
developing them. They communicate with medical and healthcare professionals about
participants' conditions using correct medical terminology, and with an understanding of
the standard treatment options for an individual’s condition. Sport therapists take a holistic
approach to the wellness of their clients that includes advising on lifestyle, food or time
management. They do not have a medical background and do not require medical
qualifications.
