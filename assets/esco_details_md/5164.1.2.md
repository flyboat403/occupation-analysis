## Alternative Terms

- kennel worker
- pet caretaker
- pound attendant
- kennel attendant
- kennel keeper

## Description

Kennel workers handle animals in kennels or catteries and provide care for pets. They feed the animals, clean their cages, look after ill or old animals, groom them and take them out for a walk.
