Description
Food graders inspect, sort and grade food products. They grade food products according to
sensory criteria or with the help of machinery. They determine the product's use by
grading them into the appropriate classes and discarding damaged or expired foods. Food
graders measure and weigh the products and report their findings so the food can be
further processed.
