Description
Marine electricians install, maintain and repair electrical and electronic systems in vessels
such as air conditioning systems, lamps, radios, heating systems, batteries, electrical
wiring and alternators. They use diagnostic testing equipment to inspect vessels and find
faults. To do repair work, they use hand tools and specialised electrical instruments and
machines.
