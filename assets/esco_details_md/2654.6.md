Description
Art administrators are among the professionals that manage the business needs of an
organisation devoted to arts. They provide support for financial and corporative tasks, and
they can work for a range of organizations from for-profit to governmental or non-profit
organisations. Examples of these organisations are dance companies, film studios, theaters
and art museums or galleries.
