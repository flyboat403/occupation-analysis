Description
Civil drafters draw and prepare sketches for civil engineers and architects of architectonic
projects of different kinds, topographical maps, or for the reconstruction of existing
structures. They lay down in the sketches all the specifications and requirements such as
mathematical, aesthetic, engineering, and technical.
