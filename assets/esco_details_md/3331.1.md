Description
Customs and excise officers approve or deny the passage of goods through customs
barriers for international business and ensure compliance with consignment legislation.
They facilitate communication between import and export trading institutions and
government officials, and are responsible for taxation calculation and ensuring payment.
Scope note
Excludes customs officer.
