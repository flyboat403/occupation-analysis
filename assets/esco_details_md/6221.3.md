Description
Aquaculture hatchery managers plan, direct, and coordinate the production in large-scale
aquaculture operations to breed fish and shellfish, developing aquaculture breeding
strategies using various types of spawning techniques. They control the reproduction and
the early life cycle stages of cultured species. They supervise incubation, early feeding and
rearing techniques of the cultured species.
