Description
Psychology lecturers are subject professors, teachers, or lecturers who instruct students
who have obtained an upper secondary education diploma in their own specialised field of
study, psychology, which is predominantly academic in nature. They work with their
university research assistants and university teaching assistants for the preparation of
lectures and of exams, for grading papers and exams and for leading review and feedback
sessions for the students. They also conduct academic research in their respective field of
psychology, publish their findings and liaise with other university colleagues.
