Description
Healthcare institution managers supervise the operations of healthcare institutions, such
as hospitals, rehabilitation facilities, home care services and elderly care institutions to
ensure the organisation meets requirements, the patients and residents are cared for, the
organisation is maintained and the necessary equipment present. They also supervise the
staff and ensure record maintenance.
