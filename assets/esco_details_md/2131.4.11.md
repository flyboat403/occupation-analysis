Description
Physiologists study and exert research on the functioning of different living organisms, the
parts they are composed of, and their interactions. They understand the fashion in which
living systems react to factors such as diseases, physical activity, and stress, and use that
information to develop methods and solutions to even out the effect that those stimuli have
in living bodies.
