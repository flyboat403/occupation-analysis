Description
Economic development coordinators outline and implement policies for the improvement of
a community's, government's or institution's economic growth and stability. They research
economic trends and coordinate cooperation between institutions working in economic
development. They analyse potential economic risks and conflicts and develop plans to
resolve them. Economic development coordinators advise on the economic sustainability of
institutions and economic growth.
