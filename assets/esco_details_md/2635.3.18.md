Description
Migrant social workers provide advice to migrants in order to guide them through the
necessary steps of integration, namely living and working in a foreign country. They
explain eligibility criteria, rights, and duties. They help migrants in the development and
maintenance of their information as clients for further referral to day care, social services
and employment programs. Migrant social workers collaborate with employers and inform
them of the available migrant services, advocating for migrant clients.
