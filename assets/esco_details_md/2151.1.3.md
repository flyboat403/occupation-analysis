Description
Electromechanical engineers design and develop equipment and machinery that use both
electrical and mechanical technology. They make draughts and prepare documents
detailing the material requisitions, the assembly process and other technical specifications.
Electromechanical engineers also test and evaluate the prototypes. They oversee the
manufacturing process.
