Description
Manufacturing cost estimators collect and analyse data to evaluate the money, materials,
labour and time required for manufacturing processes. They conduct analyses to identify
(alternative) cost effective technical designs and production processes. They develop and
use methods and tools for cost planning, controlling and analysis. They also perform
quantitative and qualitative risk analyses and report on the development of costs.
