Description
Civil enforcement officers patrol the streets to enforce parking restrictions, ensure a free
flow of traffic is maintained, ensure the safety of pedestrians, and ensure traffic and
parking laws are complied with. They aid with the combating of crime and crime reduction
by providing assistance during incidents and providing patrol services.
Scope note
Includes crossing guards.
