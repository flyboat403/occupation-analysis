Description
Grinding machine operators set up, program and control grinding machines, designed to
apply abrasive processes in order to remove small amounts of excess material and
smoothen metal workpieces by using an abrasive wheel with diamond teeth as a cutting
device for very precise and light cuts. They read grinding machine blueprints and tooling
instructions, perform regular machine maintenance, and make adjustments to the grinding
controls, such as the depth of cuts and the rotation speed.
