Description
Supreme court judges preside in high courts, dealing with complex criminal and civil cases.
They examine the case during trials in order to formulate a sentence or to direct a jury in
reaching a conclusion, and decide on any punishments if an offending party is found guilty.
They rule proceedings and ensure the trial is held in a fair manner compliant with
legislation.
