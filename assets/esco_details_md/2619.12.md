Description
Regulatory affairs managers are in charge of regulatory and legal affairs in several sectors
such as the healthcare, energy and banking industries. They oversee the development of
products and services from inception to market release by making sure everything
complies with the local legislation and meets regulatory requirements. They have
experience in the different phases of regulatory processes and act as an interface between
business and government legislation or regulatory boards.
