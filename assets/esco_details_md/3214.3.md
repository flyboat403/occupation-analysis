Description
Prosthetist-orthotists design and custom fit prostheses and orthoses for individuals who are
missing a limb from accident, disease or congenital conditions or for individuals who have
impairments, deficiences or weaknesses due to injury, pathology or congenital
malformation. They mix patient care with the design and fabrication of these devices to
address their patients' needs.
