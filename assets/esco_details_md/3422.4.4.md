Description
Ski instructors teach individuals or groups to ski and advanced skiing techniques. They
advise their students on the choice of equipment, instruct skiers in alpine safety rules and
plan and prepare ski instruction. Ski instructors demonstrate exercises and techniques
during ski lessons and give feedback to their students on how to improve their level.
