Description
Hydrologists research and study the quality, current challenges, and distribution of water
in the Earth. They study water supply from rivers, streams, and springs to determine their
adequate and sustainable use. Together with a cross functional team of professionals, they
plan and develop how water can be supplied to cities and urban areas while ensuring
efficiency and resource conservation.
