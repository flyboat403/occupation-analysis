Description
Music arrangers create arrangements for music after its creation by a composer. They
interpret, adapt or rework a composition for other instruments or voices, or to another
style. Music arrangers are experts in instruments and orchestration, harmony, polyphony
and composition techniques.
Scope note
Excludes composer.
