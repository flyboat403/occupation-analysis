Description
Textile product developers innovate and perform product design of apparel textiles, home
textiles, and technical textiles (e.g. agriculture, safety, construction, medicine, mobile tech,
environmental protection, sports, etc.). They apply scientific and technical principles to
develop innovative textile products.
