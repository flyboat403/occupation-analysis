Description
Water treatment systems operators treat water to ensure safety for drinking, irrigation, or
other use. They operate and maintain water treatment equipment and ensure the water is
safe for bottling and use in food production by thoroughly testing before distribution, and
by meeting environmental standards.
Scope note
Excludes water plant technician.
