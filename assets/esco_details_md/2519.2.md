Description
ICT auditor managers monitor ICT auditors responsible for auditing information systems,
platforms, and operating procedures in accordance with established corporate standards
for efficiency, accuracy and security. They evaluate ICT infrastructure in terms of risk to
the organisation and establish controls to mitigate loss. They determine and recommend
improvements in the current risk management controls and in the implementation of
system changes or upgrades.
