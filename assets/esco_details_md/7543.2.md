Description
Automotive test drivers drive prototype and pre-production vehicles and assess their
performance, safety and comfort. They test the models in various driving situations and
prepare reports to help engineers improve their designs and identify problems. They can
work for manufacturers, independent vehicle test organisations or automotive magazines.
