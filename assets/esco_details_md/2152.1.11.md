Description
Optoelectronic engineers design and develop optoelectronic systems and devices, such as
UV sensors, photodiodes, and LEDs. Optoelectronic engineering combines optical
engineering with electronic engineering in the design of these systems and devices. They
conduct research, perform analysis, test the devices, and supervise the research.
