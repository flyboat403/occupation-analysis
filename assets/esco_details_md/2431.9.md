Description
Hospitality revenue managers maximise revenue generated from facilities such as hotels,
holiday resorts and camping grounds by analysing trends and competition. They assist
establishment managers in strategic decisions. Hospitality revenue managers analyse and
optimise the financial potential of facilities and manage the corresponding staff.
