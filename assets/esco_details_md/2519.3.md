Description
ICT change and configuration managers organise and implement a process to manage
changes throughout the lifecycle of ICT assets such as software, applications, ICT systems,
etc. ICT change and configuration managers have a sound knowledge of the main
technologies and processes used in system engineering and to manage the lifecycle of ICT
systems and subsystems.
