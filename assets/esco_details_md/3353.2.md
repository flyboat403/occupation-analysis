Description
Social security officers advise clients on social security benefits and ensure they claim the
benefits they are eligible for, as well as providing advice on promotions and other available
support services such as employment benefits. They aid clients in applications for benefits
such as sickness, maternity, pensions, invalidity, unemployment and family benefits. They
investigate the client's right to benefits by reviewing their case and researching legislation
and the claim, and suggest an appropriate course of action. Social security advisers also
determine the aspects of a specific benefit.
