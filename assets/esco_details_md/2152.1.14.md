Description
Satellite engineers develop, test and oversee the manufacture of satellite systems and
satellite programmes. They may also develop software programs, collect and research
data, and test the satellite systems. Satellite engineers can also develop systems to
command and control satellites. They monitor satellites for issues and report on the
behaviour of the satellite in orbit.
