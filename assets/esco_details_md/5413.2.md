Description
Prison officers supervise inmates in a correctional facility and ensure the security and
peace-keeping in the facility. They perform checks and searches to ensure compliance to
regulations, monitor visitations and the activities of inmates as well as participate in
programs of rehabilitation, and ensure records maintenance.
