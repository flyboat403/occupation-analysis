Description
Asphalt plant operators extract raw materials such as sand and stones and operate mobile
equipment for their transport to the plant. They tend automated machines to crush and
sort out stones, and to mix the sand and stones with asphalt cement. They take samples to
check the quality of the mix and arrange for its transport to the construction site.
