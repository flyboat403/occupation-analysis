Description
ICT research managers plan, manage and monitor research activities and evaluate
emerging trends in the information and communication technology field to assess their
relevance. They also design and oversee staff training on the use of new technology and
recommend ways to implement new products and solutions that will maximise benefits for
the organisation.
Scope note
Includes people performing managerial research activities. Excludes people performing
development activities.
