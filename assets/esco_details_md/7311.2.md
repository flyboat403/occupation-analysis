Description
Optical instrument assemblers read blueprints and assembly drawings to assemble lenses
and optical instruments, such as microscopes, telescopes, projection equipment, and
medical diagnostic equipment. They process, grind, polish, and coat glass materials, centre
lenses according to the optical axis, and cement them to the optical frame. They may test
the instruments after assembly.
