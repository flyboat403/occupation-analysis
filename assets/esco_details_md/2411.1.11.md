Description
Public finance accountants head the treasury department of a governmental institution.
They manage the institution's financial administration, expenditure and income generation,
and compliance with taxation and other financial legislation. They perform administrative
duties to ensure record keeping, develop plans for budget management and perform
financial forecasts.
