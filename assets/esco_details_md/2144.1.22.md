Description
Tooling engineers design new tools for manufacturing equipment. They prepare tooling
quotation requests. They estimate costs and delivery time, manage tooling construction
follow-up and supervise the routine maintenance of tools. They also analyse data to
determine the cause of major tooling difficulties and develop recommendations and action
plans for solutions.
