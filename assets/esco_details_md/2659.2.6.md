Description
Variety artists are multi-disciplinary artists who master in at least two of the following
disciplines: comedy, dance, singing, circus arts, object manipulation and illusionism. They
perform solo or collectively, may appear in music variety shows, cabaret, musicals and
other entertainment events. Their artistic performance is characterised by the blending of
arts, styles and disciplines.
