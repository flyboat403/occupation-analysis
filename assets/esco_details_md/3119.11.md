Description
Offshore renewable energy technicians install offshore energy farms and equipment. They
ensure the equipment operate in compliance with regulations and assist the offshore
renewable energy engineers in the construction of energy devices such as wind turbine
blades, tidal stream and wave generators. They also react to system problems, and repair
faults.
