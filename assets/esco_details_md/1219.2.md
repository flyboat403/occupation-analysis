Description
Corporate social responsibility managers monitor the practices of organisations and
companies with regard to ethics and impact on the larger community. They advise on social
responsibility and sustainability matters depending on the company's needs. Corporate
social responsibility managers promote actions that are environmentally conscious,
philanthropic or related to human rights.
