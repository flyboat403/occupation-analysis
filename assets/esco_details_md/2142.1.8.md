Description
Rail project engineers maintain a safe, cost-effective, high-quality, and environmentally
responsible approach across the technical projects in railway companies. They provide
project management advice on all construction projects including testing, commissioning
and site supervision. They audit contractors for safety, environment and quality of design,
process and performance as to ensure that all projects follow in-house standards and
relevant legislation.
