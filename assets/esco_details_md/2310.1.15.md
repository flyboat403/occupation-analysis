Description
Education studies lecturers are professors, teachers, or lecturers who instruct students
who have obtained an upper secondary education diploma in their own specialised field of
study, education studies, so they can become teachers themselves. They work with their
university research assistants and university teaching assistants for the preparation of
lectures and of exams, grading papers and exams and leading review and feedback
sessions for the students.
Scope note
Includes people performing academic research in their field of education studies.
