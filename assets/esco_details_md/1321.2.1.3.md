Description
Food production managers oversee and monitor production and have overall responsibility
for staffing and related issues. Hence, they have a detailed knowledge of the
manufacturing products and their production processes. On the one hand, they control
process parameters and their influence on the product and on the other hand, they ensure
that staffing and recruitment levels are adequate.
Scope note
Excludes people working in small companies
