Description
Education administrators organise and manage the administration, support systems and
(student) activities of an education institution. They perform a range of administrative,
secretarial, financial and otherwise supportive tasks to enable the efficient and cost-
effective running of the school. They may assist in student recruitment, alumni relations,
funding, work on committees including academic boards and quality insurance.
Scope note
Includes executive secretary.
