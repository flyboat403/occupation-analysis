# 49-2093.00 - Electrical and Electronics Installers and Repairers, Transportation Equipment

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 49-2093.00
- Also called: Critical Systems Technician, Electronic Bench Technician, Electronics Mechanic, Locomotive Electrician, Power Technician (Power Tech), Ship Yard Electrical Person

## Summary

Install, adjust, or maintain mobile electronics communication equipment, including sound, sonar, security, navigation, and surveillance systems on trains, watercraft, or other mobile equipment.
