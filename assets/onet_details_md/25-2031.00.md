# 25-2031.00 - Secondary School Teachers, Except Special and Career/Technical Education

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 25-2031.00
- Also called: Art Teacher, English Teacher, Math Teacher (Mathematics Teacher), Music Teacher, PE Teacher (Physical Education Teacher), Science Teacher, Secondary Teacher, Social Studies Teacher, Spanish Teacher, Teacher

## Summary

Teach one or more subjects to students at the secondary school level.
