# 19-1032.00 - Foresters

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 19-1032.00
- Also called: Area Forester, District Forester, Fire Prevention Forester, Forest Practices Field Coordinator, Forester, Procurement Forester, Service Forester, Silviculturist, Timber Marker, Timber Sales Administrator (Timber Sales Admin)

## Summary

Manage public and private forested lands for economic, recreational, and conservation purposes. May inventory the type, amount, and location of standing timber, appraise the timber's worth, negotiate the purchase, and draw up contracts for procurement. May determine how to conserve wildlife habitats, creek beds, water quality, and soil stability, and how best to comply with environmental regulations. May devise plans for planting and growing new trees, monitor trees for healthy growth, and determine optimal harvesting schedules.
