# 47-4071.00 - Septic Tank Servicers and Sewer Pipe Cleaners

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-4071.00
- Also called: Drain Cleaner, Drain Technician, Public Works Technician, Septic Cleaner, Septic Pump Truck Driver, Septic Tank Service Technician, Service Technician, Sewer Bricklayer

## Summary

Clean and repair septic tanks, sewer lines, or drains. May patch walls and partitions of tank, replace damaged drain tile, or repair breaks in underground piping.
