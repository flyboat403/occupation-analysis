# 27-4031.00 - Camera Operators, Television, Video, and Film

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-4031.00
- Also called: Camera Operator, Cameraman, Master Control Operator (MCO), News Videographer, Production Technician, Studio Camera Operator, Television News Photographer, Videographer

## Summary

Operate television, video, or film camera to record images or scenes for television, video, or film productions.
