# 29-2099.08 - Patient Representatives

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 29-2099.08
- Also called: Access Representative, Admissions Coordinator, Case Manager, Medicaid Service Coordinator (MSC), Patient Access Coordinator, Patient Access Specialist, Patient Advocate, Patient Navigator, Patient Resource Worker, Patient Service Representative

## Summary

Assist patients in obtaining services, understanding policies and making health care decisions.
