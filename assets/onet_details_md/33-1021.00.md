# 33-1021.00 - First-Line Supervisors of Firefighting and Prevention Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 33-1021.00
- Also called: Engine Boss, Fire Battalion Chief, Fire Captain, Fire Chief, Fire Lieutenant, Fire Marshal, Fire Prevention Chief, Fire Suppression Captain, Forest Fire Specialist Supervisor, Section Forest Fire Warden

## Summary

Directly supervise and coordinate activities of workers engaged in firefighting and fire prevention and control.
