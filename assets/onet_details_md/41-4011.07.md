# 41-4011.07 - Solar Sales Representatives and Assessors

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 41-4011.07
- Also called: Energy Consultant, Sales Associate, Sales Consultant, Sales Representative (Sales Rep), Salesman, Solar Consultant, Solar Energy Consultant, Solar Sales Consultant, Solar Sales Representative (Solar Sales Rep), Solar Sales Specialist

## Summary

Contact new or existing customers to determine their solar equipment needs, suggest systems or equipment, or estimate costs.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 94 | Core | Prepare proposals, quotes, contracts, or presentations for potential solar customers. |

| 90 | Core | Provide customers with information, such as quotes, orders, sales, shipping, warranties, credit, funding options, incentives, or tax rebates. |
| 89 | Core | Gather information from prospective customers to identify their solar energy needs. |
