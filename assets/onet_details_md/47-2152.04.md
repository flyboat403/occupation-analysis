# 47-2152.04 - Solar Thermal Installers and Technicians

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-2152.04
- Also called: Installer, Solar Energy Technician, Solar Hot Water Installer (SHW Installer), Solar Installer, Solar Maintenance Technician, Solar System Installer, Solar Technician, Solar Thermal Installer

## Summary

Install or repair solar energy systems designed to collect, store, and circulate solar-heated water for residential, commercial or industrial use.
