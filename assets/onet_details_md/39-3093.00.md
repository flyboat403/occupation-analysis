# 39-3093.00 - Locker Room, Coatroom, and Dressing Room Attendants

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 39-3093.00
- Also called: Athletic Equipment Manager, Coat Check Attendant, Coat Checker, Coat Room Attendant, Fitting Room Attendant, Ladies Locker Room Attendant, Locker Room Attendant, Spa Attendant

## Summary
