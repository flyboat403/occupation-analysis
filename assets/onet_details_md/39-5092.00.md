# 39-5092.00 - Manicurists and Pedicurists

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 39-5092.00
- Also called: Manicurist, Nail Technician (Nail Tech), Pedicurist

## Summary

Clean and shape customers' fingernails and toenails. May polish or decorate nails.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 94 | Core | Clean and sanitize tools and work environment. |
| 91 | Core | Apply undercoat and clear or colored polish onto nails with brush. |
| 90 | Core | Maintain supply inventories and records of client services. |
| 89 | Core | Shape and smooth ends of nails, using scissors, files, or emery boards. |
| 88 | Core | Prepare nail cuticles with water and oil, using cuticle knives to push back cuticles and scissors or nippers to trim cuticles. |
| 86 | Core | Prepare customers' nails in soapy water, using swabs, files, and orange sticks. |

| 56 | Core | Promote and sell nail care products. |
