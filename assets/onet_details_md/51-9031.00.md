# 51-9031.00 - Cutters and Trimmers, Hand

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9031.00
- Also called: Cloth Cutter, Denim Cutter, Fabric Cutter, Finisher, Glass Cutter, Hand Cutter, Leather Cutter, Offline Cutter, Sample Cutter, Trimmer

## Summary

Use hand tools or hand-held power tools to cut and trim a variety of manufactured items, such as carpet, fabric, stone, glass, or rubber.
