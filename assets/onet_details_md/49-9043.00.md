# 49-9043.00 - Maintenance Workers, Machinery

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 49-9043.00
- Also called: Lubricator, Machine Repairer, Maintainer, Maintenance Craftsman, Maintenance Man, Maintenance Mechanic, Maintenance Technician (Maintenance Tech), Maintenance Worker, Oiler, Overhauler

## Summary

Lubricate machinery, change parts, or perform other routine machinery maintenance.
