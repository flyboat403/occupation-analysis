# 25-4011.00 - Archivists

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 25-4011.00
- Also called: Accessioning Archivist, Archivist, Digital Archivist, Film Archivist, Museum Archivist, Records Manager, Reference Archivist, Registrar, State Archivist, University Archivist

## Summary

Appraise, edit, and direct safekeeping of permanent records and historically valuable documents. Participate in research activities based on archival materials.
