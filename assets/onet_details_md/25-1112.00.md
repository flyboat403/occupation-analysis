# 25-1112.00 - Law Teachers, Postsecondary

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 25-1112.00
- Also called: Adjunct Professor, Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Business Law Professor, Clinical Law Professor, Instructor, Law Instructor, Law Professor, Legal Writing Professor, Professor

## Summary

Teach courses in law. Includes both teachers primarily engaged in teaching and those who do a combination of teaching and research.
