# 51-9071.00 - Jewelers and Precious Stone and Metal Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9071.00
- Also called: Appraiser, Artisan Jeweler, Bench Jeweler, Caster, Gemologist, Goldsmith, Jeweler, Polisher, Silversmith, Stone Setter

## Summary

Design, fabricate, adjust, repair, or appraise jewelry, gold, silver, other precious metals, or gems.
