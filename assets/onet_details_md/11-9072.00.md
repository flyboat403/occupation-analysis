# 11-9072.00 - Entertainment and Recreation Managers, Except Gambling

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-9072.00
- Also called: Camp and Recreation Manager, Camp Director, Events and Competitions Director, Events Manager, Experiences Manager, Golf Course Manager, Park Manager, Recreation Director, Recreation Superintendent, Social Activities Director

## Summary

Plan, direct, or coordinate entertainment and recreational activities and operations of a recreational facility, including cruise ships and parks.
