# 53-7041.00 - Hoist and Winch Operators

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-7041.00
- Also called: Hoist Operator, Hoistman, Material Handler, Service Operator, Winch Derrick Operator

## Summary

Operate or tend hoists or winches to lift and pull loads using power-operated cable equipment.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 90 | Core | Move levers, pedals, and throttles to stop, start, and regulate speeds of hoist or winch drums in response to hand, bell, buzzer, telephone, loud-speaker, or whistle signals, or by observing dial indicators or cable marks. |
| 88 | Core | Start engines of hoists or winches and use levers and pedals to wind or unwind cable on drums. |
| 86 | Core | Observe equipment gauges and indicators and hand signals of other workers to verify load positions or depths. |
| 84 | Core | Operate compressed air, diesel, electric, gasoline, or steam-driven hoists or winches to control movement of cableways, cages, derricks, draglines, loaders, railcars, or skips. |
