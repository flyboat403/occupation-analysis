# 11-9071.00 - Gambling Managers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-9071.00
- Also called: Casino Manager, Casino Operations Manager, Casino Shift Manager, Gaming Manager, Pit Manager, Shift Manager, Slot Manager, Slot Operations Manager, Table Games Manager, Table Games Shift Manager

## Summary

Plan, direct, or coordinate gambling operations in a casino. May formulate house rules.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 90 | Core | Resolve customer complaints regarding problems, such as payout errors. |
| 87 | Core | Remove suspected cheaters, such as card counters or other players who may have systems that shift the odds of winning to their favor. |
| 84 | Core | Track supplies of money to tables and perform any required paperwork. |
| 84 | Core | Explain and interpret house rules, such as game rules or betting limits. |
| 82 | Core | Prepare work schedules and station arrangements and keep attendance records. |
| 82 | Core | Monitor staffing levels to ensure that games and tables are adequately staffed for each shift, arranging for staff rotations and breaks and locating substitute employees as necessary. |
| 82 | Core | Maintain familiarity with all games used at a facility, as well as strategies or tricks employed in those games. |
| 81 | Core | Train new workers or evaluate their performance. |
| 80 | Core | Market or promote the casino to bring in business. |
| 79 | Core | Interview and hire workers. |
| 78 | Core | Direct the distribution of complimentary hotel rooms, meals, or other discounts or free items given to players, based on their length of play and betting totals. |
| 76 | Core | Establish policies on issues, such as the type of gambling offered and the odds, the extension of credit, or the serving of food and beverages. |
| 86 | Supplemental | Circulate among gaming tables to ensure that operations are conducted properly, that dealers follow house rules, or that players are not cheating. |
| 84 | Supplemental | Set and maintain a bank and table limit for each game. |
| 82 | Supplemental | Direct the compilation of summary sheets that show wager amounts and payoffs for races or events. |
| 81 | Supplemental | Review operational expenses, budget estimates, betting accounts, or collection reports for accuracy. |

| 73 | Supplemental | Monitor credit extended to players. |
