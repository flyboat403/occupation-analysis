# 39-7011.00 - Tour Guides and Escorts

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 39-7011.00
- Also called: Art Museum Docent, Discovery Guide, Docent, Guide, Historical Interpreter, Museum Docent, Museum Educator, Museum Guide, Science Interpreter, Tour Guide

## Summary

Escort individuals or groups on sightseeing tours or through places of interest, such as industrial establishments, public buildings, and art galleries.
