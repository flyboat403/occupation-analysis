# 51-7031.00 - Model Makers, Wood

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-7031.00
- Also called: Builder, Craftsman, Jig Maker, Model Builder, Model Maker, Product Development Carpenter, Sample Builder, Sample Maker, Sample Worker, Wood Carver

## Summary

Construct full-size and scale wooden precision models of products. Includes wood jig builders and loft workers.
