# 11-3121.00 - Human Resources Managers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-3121.00
- Also called: Employee Relations Manager, HR Admin Director (Human Resources Administration Director), HR Director (Human Resources Director), HR Manager (Human Resources Manager), HR Ops Manager (Human Resources Operations Manager), HR VP (Human Resources Vice President), Recruitment Director

## Summary

Plan, direct, or coordinate human resources activities and staff of an organization.
