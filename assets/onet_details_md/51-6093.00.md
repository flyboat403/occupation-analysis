# 51-6093.00 - Upholsterers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-6093.00
- Also called: Box Spring Upholsterer, Furniture Upholsterer, Inside Upholsterer, Sofa Back Upholsterer, Stapler, Trimmer, Upholstered Goods Crafter, Upholsterer, Upholstery Cutter, Upholstery Trimmer

## Summary

Make, repair, or replace upholstery for household furniture or transportation vehicles.
