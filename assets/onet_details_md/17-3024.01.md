# 17-3024.01 - Robotics Technicians

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 17-3024.01
- Also called: Automation Technician, Electrical and Instrumentation Technician (E and I Technician), Instrument and Automation Technician, Instrument Specialist, Instrument Technician, Instrumentation and Controls Technician, Instrumentation and Process Controls Technician, Process Control Technician, Programmable Logic Controllers Technician

## Summary

Build, install, test, or maintain robotic equipment or related automated production systems.
