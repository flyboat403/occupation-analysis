# 13-2082.00 - Tax Preparers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 13-2082.00
- Also called: CPA (Certified Public Accountant), Enrolled Agent, Income Tax Preparer, Tax Accountant, Tax Advisor, Tax Associate, Tax Consultant, Tax Preparer, Tax Professional, Tax Specialist

## Summary

Prepare tax returns for individuals or small businesses.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 94 | Core | Compute taxes owed or overpaid, using adding machines or personal computers, and complete entries on forms, following tax form instructions and tax tables. |
| 93 | Core | Use all appropriate adjustments, deductions, and credits to keep clients' taxes to a minimum. |
| 89 | Core | Furnish taxpayers with sufficient information and advice to ensure correct tax form completion. |
| 89 | Core | Interview clients to obtain additional information on taxable income and deductible expenses and allowances. |
| 88 | Core | Prepare or assist in preparing simple to complex tax returns for individuals or small businesses. |
| 86 | Core | Review financial records, such as income statements and documentation of expenditures to determine forms needed to prepare tax returns. |
| 84 | Core | Check data input or verify totals on forms prepared by others to detect errors in arithmetic, data entry, or procedures. |
| 80 | Core | Explain federal and state tax laws to individuals and companies. |
| 80 | Core | Answer questions and provide future tax planning to clients. |
| 80 | Core | Consult tax law handbooks or bulletins to determine procedures for preparation of atypical returns. |
| 76 | Core | Calculate form preparation fees according to return complexity and processing time required. |
| 69 | Core | Schedule appointments with clients. |
