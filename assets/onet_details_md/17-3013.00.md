# 17-3013.00 - Mechanical Drafters

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 17-3013.00
- Also called: CAD Designer (Computer Aided Design Designer), CAD Operator (Computer Aided Design Operator), Design Drafter, Drafter, Drafting Technician, Mechanical Designer, Mechanical Drafter, Product Designer, Project Designer

## Summary

Prepare detailed working diagrams of machinery and mechanical devices, including dimensions, fastening methods, and other engineering information.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 91 | Core | Develop detailed design drawings and specifications for mechanical equipment, dies, tools, and controls, using computer-assisted drafting (CAD) equipment. |
| 86 | Core | Produce three-dimensional models, using computer-aided design (CAD) software. |
| 83 | Core | Lay out and draw schematic, orthographic, or angle views to depict functional relationships of components, assemblies, systems, and machines. |

| 80 | Core | Review and analyze specifications, sketches, drawings, ideas, and related data to assess factors affecting component designs and the procedures and instructions to be followed. |
