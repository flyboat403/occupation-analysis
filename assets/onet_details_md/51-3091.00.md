# 51-3091.00 - Food and Tobacco Roasting, Baking, and Drying Machine Operators and Tenders

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-3091.00
- Also called: Bean Roaster, Coffee Roaster, Line Operator, Machine Operator, Oven Operator, Oven Technician, Roast Master, Roaster, Roaster Operator, Roasterman

## Summary

Operate or tend food or tobacco roasting, baking, or drying equipment, including hearth ovens, kiln driers, roasters, char kilns, and vacuum drying equipment.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 90 | Core | Observe, feel, taste, or otherwise examine products during and after processing to ensure conformance to standards. |
