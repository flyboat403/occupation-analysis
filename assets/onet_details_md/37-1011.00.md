# 37-1011.00 - First-Line Supervisors of Housekeeping and Janitorial Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 37-1011.00
- Also called: Building Services Supervisor, Buildings and Grounds Supervisor, Custodian Supervisor, Environmental Services Supervisor (EVS), Executive Housekeeper, Housekeeping Supervisor, Janitorial Supervisor, Laundry Supervisor, Maintenance Supervisor

## Summary

Directly supervise and coordinate work activities of cleaning personnel in hotels, hospitals, offices, and other establishments.
