# 51-9193.00 - Cooling and Freezing Equipment Operators and Tenders

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9193.00
- Also called: Certified Refrigeration Operator, Compressor Operator, Engine Room Operator, Freezer Operator, Freezer Person, Ice Cream Maker, Machine Operator, Refrigeration Operator, Refrigeration Technician

## Summary

Operate or tend equipment such as cooling and freezing units, refrigerators, batch freezers, and freezing tunnels, to cool or freeze products, food, blood plasma, and chemicals.
