# 53-1042.00 - First-Line Supervisors of Helpers, Laborers, and Material Movers, Hand

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-1042.00
- Also called: Floor Supervisor, Packaging Supervisor, Receiving Manager, Receiving Supervisor, Shipping Manager, Shipping Supervisor, Terminal Operations Manager, Warehouse Foreman, Warehouse Manager, Warehouse Supervisor

## Summary

Directly supervise and coordinate the activities of helpers, laborers, or material movers, hand.
