# 51-9123.00 - Painting, Coating, and Decorating Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9123.00
- Also called: Decaler, Decorator, Glass Decorator, Glazer, In Mold Coater, Painter, Pottery Decorator, Silk-Screen Operator, Spray Painter, Sprayer

## Summary

Paint, coat, or decorate articles, such as furniture, glass, plateware, pottery, jewelry, toys, books, or leather.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |

| 72 | Supplemental | Rinse, drain, or wipe coated workpieces to remove excess coating material or to facilitate setting of finish coats on workpieces. |
