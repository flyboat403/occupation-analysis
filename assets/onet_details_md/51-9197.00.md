# 51-9197.00 - Tire Builders

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9197.00
- Also called: Buffer, Recapper, Retread Associate, Retread Technician, Retreader, Tire Assembler, Tire Builder, Tire Retreader, Tire Technician, Tread Builder Operator

## Summary

Operate machines to build tires.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 90 | Core | Build semi-raw rubber treads onto buffed tire casings to prepare tires for vulcanization in recapping or retreading processes. |
| 87 | Core | Trim excess rubber and imperfections during retreading processes. |
| 85 | Core | Fill cuts and holes in tires, using hot rubber. |
| 96 | Supplemental | Place tires into molds for new tread. |
| 96 | Supplemental | Fit inner tubes and final layers of rubber onto tires. |
| 93 | Supplemental | Buff tires according to specifications for width and undertread depth. |
| 90 | Supplemental | Brush or spray solvents onto plies to ensure adhesion, and repeat process as specified, alternating direction of each ply to strengthen tires. |
| 89 | Supplemental | Start rollers that bond tread and plies as drums revolve. |
| 89 | Supplemental | Align treads with guides, start drums to wind treads onto plies, and slice ends. |
| 89 | Supplemental | Inspect worn tires for faults, cracks, cuts, and nail holes, and to determine if tires are suitable for retreading. |
| 88 | Supplemental | Measure tires to determine mold size requirements. |
| 85 | Supplemental | Roll hand rollers over rebuilt casings, exerting pressure to ensure adhesion between camelbacks and casings. |
| 84 | Supplemental | Position ply stitcher rollers and drums according to width of stock, using hand tools and gauges. |
| 84 | Supplemental | Cut plies at splice points, and press ends together to form continuous bands. |
| 83 | Supplemental | Depress pedals to rotate drums, and wind specified numbers of plies around drums to form tire bodies. |
| 82 | Supplemental | Clean and paint completed tires. |
| 79 | Supplemental | Rub cement sticks on drum edges to provide adhesive surfaces for plies. |
| 76 | Supplemental | Depress pedals to collapse drums after processing is complete. |
| 74 | Supplemental | Wind chafers and breakers onto plies. |
| 61 | Supplemental | Pull plies from supply racks, and align plies with edges of drums. |
