# 41-3041.00 - Travel Agents

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 41-3041.00
- Also called: Auto Travel Counselor, Beach Expert, Corporate Travel Consultant, Destination Specialist, International Travel Consultant, Tour Coordinator, Tour Counselor, Travel Agent, Travel Consultant, Travel Counselor

## Summary

Plan and sell transportation and accommodations for customers. Determine destination, modes of transportation, travel dates, costs, and accommodations required. May also describe, plan, and arrange itineraries and sell tour packages. May assist in resolving clients' travel problems.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|

| 89 | Core | Plan, describe, arrange, and sell itinerary tour packages and promotional travel incentives offered by various travel carriers. |

| 89 | Core | Compute cost of travel and accommodations, using calculator, computer, carrier tariff books, and hotel rate books, or quote package tour's costs. |
| 87 | Core | Record and maintain information on clients, vendors, and travel packages. |

| 76 | Core | Provide customer with brochures and publications containing travel information, such as local customs, points of interest, or foreign country regulations. |
