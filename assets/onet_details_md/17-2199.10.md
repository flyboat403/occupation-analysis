# 17-2199.10 - Wind Energy Engineers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 17-2199.10
- Also called: Engineer, Project Engineer, Turbine Measurements Engineer, Utility Engineer, Wind Energy Consultant, Wind Farm Siting and Development Consultant, Wind Turbine Design Engineer

## Summary

Design underground or overhead wind farm collector systems and prepare and develop site specifications.
