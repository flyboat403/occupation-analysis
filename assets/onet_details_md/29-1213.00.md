# 29-1213.00 - Dermatologists

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 29-1213.00
- Also called: Board Certified Dermatologist, Dermatologist MD (Dermatologist Medical Doctor), Dermatologist Physician, Dermatopathologist, Doctor, MD (Medical Doctor), Mohs Micrographic Surgeon, Mohs Surgeon, Pediatric Dermatologist, Practicing Dermatologist

## Summary

Diagnose and treat diseases relating to the skin, hair, and nails. May perform both medical and dermatological surgery functions.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 98 | Core | Conduct complete skin examinations. |
| 97 | Core | Diagnose and treat pigmented lesions such as common acquired nevi, congenital nevi, dysplastic nevi, Spitz nevi, blue nevi, or melanoma. |
