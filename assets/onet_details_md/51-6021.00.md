# 51-6021.00 - Pressers, Textile, Garment, and Related Materials

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-6021.00
- Also called: Boarder, Dry Cleaner Presser, Garment Presser, Ironing Machine Operator, Ironing Worker, Pants Presser, Presser, Pressing Machine Operator, Shirt Presser, Silk Presser

## Summary

Press or shape articles by hand or machine.
