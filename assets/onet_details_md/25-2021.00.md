# 25-2021.00 - Elementary School Teachers, Except Special Education

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 25-2021.00
- Also called: Art Teacher, Classroom Teacher, Elementary Classroom Teacher, Elementary School Teacher, Elementary Teacher, Math Teacher (Mathematics Teacher), Music Teacher, Primary Teacher, Reading Teacher, Teacher

## Summary

Teach academic and social skills to students at the elementary school level.
