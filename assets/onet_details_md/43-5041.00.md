# 43-5041.00 - Meter Readers, Utilities

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 43-5041.00
- Also called: Field Technician, Fieldman, Meter Reader, Meter Reader Inspector, Meter Reading Clerk, Meter Technician, Utility Service Worker, Water Inspector, Water Meter Reader, Water Use Inspector

## Summary

Read meter and record consumption of electricity, gas, water, or steam.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 87 | Core | Read electric, gas, water, or steam consumption meters and enter data in route books or hand-held computers. |
| 82 | Core | Upload into office computers all information collected on hand-held computers during meter rounds, or return route books or hand-held computers to business offices so that data can be compiled. |
| 81 | Core | Walk or drive vehicles along established routes to take readings of meter dials. |
| 80 | Core | Inspect meters for unauthorized connections, defects, and damage, such as broken seals. |
| 80 | Core | Verify readings in cases where consumption appears to be abnormal, and record possible reasons for fluctuations. |
| 78 | Core | Report to service departments any problems, such as meter irregularities, damaged equipment, or impediments to meter access, including dogs. |
| 76 | Core | Leave messages to arrange different times to read meters in cases in which meters are not accessible. |
| 74 | Core | Connect and disconnect utility services at specific locations. |
| 74 | Core | Answer customers' questions about services and charges, or direct them to customer service centers. |
| 70 | Core | Update client address and meter location information. |
| 67 | Core | Perform preventative maintenance or minor repairs on meters. |
| 68 | Supplemental | Report lost or broken keys. |
