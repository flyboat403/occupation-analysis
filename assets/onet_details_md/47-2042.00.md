# 47-2042.00 - Floor Layers, Except Carpet, Wood, and Hard Tiles

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-2042.00
- Also called: Floor Covering Contractor, Floor Coverings Installer, Floor Layer, Flooring Installer, Flooring Mechanic, Tile Installer, Tile Setter, Vinyl Installer

## Summary

Apply blocks, strips, or sheets of shock-absorbing, sound-deadening, or decorative coverings to floors.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 81 | Core | Sweep, scrape, sand, or chip dirt and irregularities to clean base surfaces, correcting imperfections that may show through the covering. |
| 81 | Core | Cut flooring material to fit around obstructions. |
| 79 | Core | Inspect surface to be covered to ensure that it is firm and dry. |
| 77 | Core | Trim excess covering materials, tack edges, and join sections of covering material to form tight joint. |
| 76 | Core | Form a smooth foundation by stapling plywood or Masonite over the floor or by brushing waterproof compound onto surface and filling cracks with plaster, putty, or grout to seal pores. |
| 76 | Core | Measure and mark guidelines on surfaces or foundations, using chalk lines and dividers. |
| 75 | Core | Cut covering and foundation materials, according to blueprints and sketches. |
| 74 | Core | Roll and press sheet wall and floor covering into cement base to smooth and finish surface, using hand roller. |
| 74 | Core | Apply adhesive cement to floor or wall material to join and adhere foundation material. |
| 72 | Core | Determine traffic areas and decide location of seams. |
| 72 | Core | Lay out, position, and apply shock-absorbing, sound-deadening, or decorative coverings to floors, walls, and cabinets, following guidelines to keep courses straight and create designs. |
| 66 | Core | Remove excess cement to clean finished surface. |
| 57 | Core | Disconnect and remove appliances, light fixtures, and worn floor and wall covering from floors, walls, and cabinets. |
| 63 | Supplemental | Heat and soften floor covering materials to patch cracks or fit floor coverings around irregular surfaces, using blowtorch. |
