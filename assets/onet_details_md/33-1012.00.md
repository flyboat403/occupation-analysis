# 33-1012.00 - First-Line Supervisors of Police and Detectives

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 33-1012.00
- Also called: Captain, Deputy Sheriff, Detective Sergeant, Lieutenant, Patrol Sergeant, Police Captain, Police Chief, Police Lieutenant, Police Sergeant, Shift Supervisor

## Summary

Directly supervise and coordinate activities of members of police force.
