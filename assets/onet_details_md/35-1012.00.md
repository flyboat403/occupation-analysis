# 35-1012.00 - First-Line Supervisors of Food Preparation and Serving Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 35-1012.00
- Also called: Cafeteria Manager, Dietary Supervisor, Dining Services Director, Food and Beverage Director, Food and Nutrition Services Supervisor, Food Production Supervisor, Food Service Supervisor, Kitchen Manager, Kitchen Supervisor, Restaurant Manager

## Summary

Directly supervise and coordinate activities of workers engaged in preparing and serving food.
