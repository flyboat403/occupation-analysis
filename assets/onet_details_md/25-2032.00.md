# 25-2032.00 - Career/Technical Education Teachers, Secondary School

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 25-2032.00
- Also called: Agricultural Education Teacher, Auto Technology Instructor (Automotive Technology Instructor), Career and Technical Education Teacher (CTE Teacher), Cosmetology Teacher, Family and Consumer Sciences Teacher (FACS Teacher), Instructor, Teacher, Technology Education Teacher, Vocational Teacher, Welding Instructor

## Summary

Teach occupational, vocational, career, or technical subjects to students at the secondary school level.
