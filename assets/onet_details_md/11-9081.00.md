# 11-9081.00 - Lodging Managers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-9081.00
- Also called: Bed and Breakfast Innkeeper, Front Desk Manager, Front Office Director, Front Office Manager, Guest Relations Manager, Guest Services Manager, Hotel Manager, Night Manager, Resort Manager, Rooms Director

## Summary

Plan, direct, or coordinate activities of an organization or department that provides lodging and other accommodations.
