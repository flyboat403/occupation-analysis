# 41-9091.00 - Door-to-Door Sales Workers, News and Street Vendors, and Related Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 41-9091.00
- Also called: Direct Sales Coach, Door-to-Door Sales Trainer, Independent Beauty Consultant, Independent Distributor, Independent Sales Associate, Independent Sales Representative, Sales Representative (Sales Rep), Street Vendor

## Summary

Sell goods or services door-to-door or on the street.
