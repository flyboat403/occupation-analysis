# 11-9031.00 - Education and Childcare Administrators, Preschool and Daycare

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-9031.00
- Also called: Childcare Director, Early Head Start Director, Education Coordinator, Education Director, Education Site Manager, Preschool Director, Preschool Program Director, Principal, Site Coordinator

## Summary

Plan, direct, or coordinate academic or nonacademic activities of preschools or childcare centers and programs, including before- and after-school care.
