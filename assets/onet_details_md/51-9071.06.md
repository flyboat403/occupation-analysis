# 51-9071.06 - Gem and Diamond Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9071.06
- Also called: Diamond Cutter, Diamond Grader, Diamond Picker, Diamond Polisher, Diamond Sawer, Diamond Setter, Facetor, Gemologist, Lapidarist

## Summary

Fabricate, finish, or evaluate the quality of gems and diamonds used in jewelry or industrial tools.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 92 | Core | Examine gems during processing to ensure accuracy of angles and positions of cuts or bores, using magnifying glasses, loupes, or shadowgraphs. |
| 91 | Core | Assign polish, symmetry, and clarity grades to stones, according to established grading systems. |
| 85 | Core | Estimate wholesale and retail value of gems, following pricing guides, market fluctuations, and other relevant economic factors. |
| 83 | Core | Examine gem surfaces and internal structures, using polariscopes, refractometers, microscopes, and other optical instruments, to differentiate between stones, to identify rare specimens, or to detect flaws, defects, or peculiarities affecting gem values. |
| 81 | Core | Identify and document stones' clarity characteristics, using plot diagrams. |
| 72 | Core | Advise customers and others on the best use of gems to create attractive jewelry items. |
| 86 | Supplemental | Examine diamonds or gems to ascertain the shape, cut, and width of cut stones, or to select the cuts that will result in the biggest, best quality stones. |
| 85 | Supplemental | Immerse stones in prescribed chemical solutions to determine specific gravities and key properties of gemstones or substitutes. |
| 84 | Supplemental | Hold stones, gems, dies, or styluses against rotating plates, wheels, saws, or slitters to cut, shape, slit, grind, or polish them. |
| 83 | Supplemental | Sort rough diamonds into categories based on shape, size, color, and quality. |
| 81 | Supplemental | Secure gems or diamonds in holders, chucks, dops, lapidary sticks, or blocks for cutting, polishing, grinding, drilling, or shaping. |
| 68 | Supplemental | Measure sizes of stones' bore holes and cuts to ensure adherence to specifications, using precision measuring instruments. |
| 65 | Supplemental | Select shaping wheels for tasks, and mix and apply abrasives, bort, or polishing compounds. |
| 55 | Supplemental | Dismantle lapping, boring, cutting, polishing, and shaping equipment and machinery to clean and lubricate it. |
