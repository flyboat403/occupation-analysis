# 19-5012.00 - Occupational Health and Safety Technicians

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 19-5012.00
- Also called: Advisory Industrial Hygienist; Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH); Construction Safety Consultant; EHS Advisor (Environmental, Health, and Safety Advisor); Health and Safety Technician (Health and Safety Tech); Industrial Hygiene Consultant; Industrial Hygienist; Safety Specialist; Safety Technician (Safety Tech); Safety Trainer

## Summary

Collect data on work environments for analysis by occupational health and safety specialists. Implement and conduct evaluation of programs designed to limit chemical, physical, biological, and ergonomic risks to workers.
