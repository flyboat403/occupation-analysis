# 33-3021.00 - Detectives and Criminal Investigators

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 33-3021.00
- Also called: Crime Scene Investigator (CSI), Criminal Investigator, Detective, Fugitive Detective, Fugitive Investigator, Investigator, Narcotics Detective, Narcotics Investigator, Police Detective, Special Agent

## Summary

Conduct investigations related to suspected violations of federal, state, or local laws to prevent or solve crimes.
