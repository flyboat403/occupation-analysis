# 51-9081.00 - Dental Laboratory Technicians

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9081.00
- Also called: Ceramist, Crown and Bridge Dental Laboratory Technician (Crown and Bridge Dental Lab Tech), Dental Ceramist, Dental Laboratory Technician (Dental Lab Tech), Dental Technician (Dental Tech), Denture Technician (Denture Tech), Metal Finisher, Orthodontic Laboratory Technician (Ortho Lab Tech), Porcelain Technician (Porcelain Tech), Waxer

## Summary

Construct and repair full or partial dentures or dental appliances.
