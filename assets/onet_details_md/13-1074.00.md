# 13-1074.00 - Farm Labor Contractors

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 13-1074.00
- Also called: Farm Labor Contractor, Field Manager, Field Supervisor

## Summary

Recruit and hire seasonal or temporary agricultural laborers. May transport, house, and provide meals for workers.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 87 | Core | Pay wages of contracted farm laborers. |
| 87 | Core | Provide food, drinking water, and field sanitation facilities to contracted workers. |
| 83 | Core | Recruit and hire agricultural workers. |
| 78 | Core | Employ foremen to deal directly with workers when recruiting, hiring, instructing, assigning tasks, and enforcing work rules. |
| 77 | Core | Supervise the work of contracted employees. |
| 76 | Core | Furnish tools for employee use. |
| 78 | Supplemental | Direct and transport workers to appropriate work sites. |
