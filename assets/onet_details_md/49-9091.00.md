# 49-9091.00 - Coin, Vending, and Amusement Machine Servicers and Repairers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 49-9091.00
- Also called: Cooler Deliverer, Field Service Technician, Fountain Vending Mechanic, Full Service Vending Driver, Refurbish Technician, Service Technician, Slot Technician, Vending Mechanic, Vending Service Technician, Vending Technician

## Summary

Install, service, adjust, or repair coin, vending, or amusement machines including video games, juke boxes, pinball machines, or slot machines.
