# 27-3041.00 - Editors

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-3041.00
- Also called: Acquisitions Editor, Business Editor, Editor, Features Editor, Legal Editor, News Editor, Newspaper Copy Editor, Science Editor, Sports Editor, Web Editor

## Summary

Plan, coordinate, revise, or edit written material. May review proposals and drafts for possible publication.
