# 27-2023.00 - Umpires, Referees, and Other Sports Officials

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-2023.00
- Also called: Basketball Referee, Diving Judge, Dressage Judge, Football Referee, Horse Show Judge, Major League Baseball Umpire (MLB Umpire), Referee, Soccer Referee, Softball Umpire, Sports Official

## Summary

Officiate at competitive athletic or sporting events. Detect infractions of rules and decide penalties according to established regulations. Includes all sporting officials, referees, and competition judges.
