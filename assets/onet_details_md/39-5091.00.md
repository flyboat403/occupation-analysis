# 39-5091.00 - Makeup Artists, Theatrical and Performance

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 39-5091.00
- Also called: Commercial Makeup Artist (Commercial MUA), Hair and Makeup Designer, Makeup Artist (MUA), Prosthetic Makeup Designer, Special Effects Makeup Artist (Special Effects MUA), Special Makeup Effects Artist, TV and Film Makeup Artist (Television and Film Makeup Artist)

## Summary

Apply makeup to performers to reflect period, setting, and situation of their role.
