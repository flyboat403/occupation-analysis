# 51-6061.00 - Textile Bleaching and Dyeing Machine Operators and Tenders

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-6061.00
- Also called: Beck Operator, Dye House Worker, Dye Line Operator, Dye Machine Operator, Dye Operator, Dye Tub Operator, Dyer, Jet Dyeing Machine Operator, Machine Operator (Machine Op), Tub Operator

## Summary

Operate or tend machines to bleach, shrink, wash, dye, or finish textiles or synthetic or glass fibers.
