# 51-9023.00 - Mixing and Blending Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9023.00
- Also called: Batchmaker, Blender, Blending Technician (Blending Tech), Ink Blender, Issuing Operator, Machine Operator, Mixer, Mixer Operator, Operator, Stock Preparation Operator (Stock Prep Operator)

## Summary

Set up, operate, or tend machines to mix or blend materials, such as chemicals, tobacco, liquids, color pigments, or explosive ingredients.
