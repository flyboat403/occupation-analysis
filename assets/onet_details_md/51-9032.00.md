# 51-9032.00 - Cutting and Slicing Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-9032.00
- Also called: Cutter, Cutter Operator, Cutting Pressman, Die Cutter Operator, Flat Cutter, Machine Operator, Paper Cutter, Sheeter, Skiver Operator, Slitter

## Summary

Set up, operate, or tend machines that cut or slice materials, such as glass, stone, cork, rubber, tobacco, food, paper, or insulating material.
