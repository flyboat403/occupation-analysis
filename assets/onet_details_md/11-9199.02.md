# 11-9199.02 - Compliance Managers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-9199.02
- Also called: Accreditation Lieutenant; Accreditation Manager; Compliance Director; Compliance Manager; Compliance Operations Manager; Environmental Health and Safety Director; Environmental Manager; Environmental Program Manager; Health, Safety, and Environmental Manager (HSE Manager); Risk Manager

## Summary

Plan, direct, or coordinate activities of an organization to ensure compliance with ethical or regulatory standards.
