# 29-1129.01 - Art Therapists

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 29-1129.01
- Also called: Art Psychotherapist, Art Therapist, Board Certified Art Therapist (ATR-BC), Creative Arts Therapist, Group Therapist, Oncology Art Therapist, Registered Art Therapist (ATR), Therapist

## Summary

Plan or conduct art therapy sessions or programs to improve clients' physical, cognitive, or emotional well-being.
