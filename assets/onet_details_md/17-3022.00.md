# 17-3022.00 - Civil Engineering Technologists and Technicians

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 17-3022.00
- Also called: Civil Designer, Civil Engineering Assistant, Civil Engineering Technician, Design Technician, Engineer Technician, Engineering Assistant, Engineering Technician, Transportation Engineering Technician

## Summary

Apply theory and principles of civil engineering in planning, designing, and overseeing construction and maintenance of structures and facilities under the direction of engineering staff or physical scientists.
