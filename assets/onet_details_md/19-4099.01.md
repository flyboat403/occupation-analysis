# 19-4099.01 - Quality Control Analysts

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 19-4099.01
- Also called: Lab Analyst, Lab Technician (Lab Tech), Laboratory Analyst, Microbiology Lab Analyst, QA Auditor (Quality Assurance Auditor), QA Lab Tech (Quality Assurance Lab Technician), QA Tech (Quality Assurance Technician), Quality Control Analyst (QC Analyst), Quality Control Lab Technician (QC Lab Tech), Quality Control Technician (QC Tech)

## Summary

Conduct tests to determine quality of raw materials, bulk intermediate and finished products. May conduct stability sample tests.
