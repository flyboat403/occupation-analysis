# 51-2022.00 - Electrical and Electronic Equipment Assemblers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-2022.00
- Also called: Assembler, Assembly Worker, Electrical Assembler, Electronic Assembler, Electronics Assembler, Factory Assembler, Manufacturing Assembler, Transformer Assembler

## Summary

Assemble or modify electrical or electronic equipment, such as computers, test equipment telemetering systems, electric motors, and batteries.
