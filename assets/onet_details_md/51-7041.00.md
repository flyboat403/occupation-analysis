# 51-7041.00 - Sawing Machine Setters, Operators, and Tenders, Wood

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-7041.00
- Also called: Bandmill Operator, Cut Off Saw Operator, Edgerman, Knot Saw Operator, Panel Saw Operator, Planer, Resaw Operator, Rip Saw Operator, Saw Operator, Sawyer

## Summary

Set up, operate, or tend wood sawing machines. May operate computer numerically controlled (CNC) equipment. Includes lead sawyers.
