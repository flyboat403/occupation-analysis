# 27-3091.00 - Interpreters and Translators

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-3091.00
- Also called: American Sign Language Interpreter (ASL Interpreter), Court Interpreter, Educational Interpreter, Interpreter, Linguist, Medical Interpreter, Sign Language Interpreter, Spanish Interpreter, Spanish Translator, Translator

## Summary

Interpret oral or sign language, or translate written text from one language into another.
