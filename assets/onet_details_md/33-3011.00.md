# 33-3011.00 - Bailiffs

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 33-3011.00
- Also called: Bailiff, Court Bailiff, Court Constable, Court Deputy, Court Officer, Court Security Officer, Deputy Bailiff, Deputy Court Services Sheriff, Security Officer

## Summary

Maintain order in courts of law.
