# 47-2082.00 - Tapers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-2082.00
- Also called: Drywall Finisher, Drywall Mechanic, Drywall Taper, Finisher, Taper

## Summary

Seal joints between plasterboard or other wallboard to prepare wall surface for painting or papering.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 83 | Core | Spread sealing compound between boards or panels or over cracks, holes, nail heads, or screw heads, using trowels, broadknives, or spatulas. |
| 82 | Core | Press paper tape over joints to embed tape into sealing compound and to seal joints. |
| 82 | Core | Apply additional coats to fill in holes and make surfaces smooth. |
| 82 | Core | Seal joints between plasterboard or other wallboard to prepare wall surfaces for painting or papering. |
| 81 | Core | Spread and smooth cementing material over tape, using trowels or floating machines to blend joints with wall surfaces. |
| 79 | Core | Sand or patch nicks or cracks in plasterboard or wallboard. |
| 78 | Core | Mix sealing compounds by hand or with portable electric mixers. |
| 78 | Core | Work on high ceilings, using scaffolding or other tools, such as stilts. |
| 76 | Core | Select the correct sealing compound or tape. |
| 74 | Core | Countersink nails or screws below surfaces of walls before applying sealing compounds, using hammers or screwdrivers. |

| 72 | Core | Install metal molding at wall corners to secure wallboard. |
| 71 | Core | Apply texturizing compounds or primers to walls or ceilings before final finishing, using trowels, brushes, rollers, or spray guns. |
