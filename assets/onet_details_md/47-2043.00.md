# 47-2043.00 - Floor Sanders and Finishers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-2043.00
- Also called: Finisher, Floor Finisher, Floor Mechanic, Floor Refinisher, Floor Sander, Floor Sander and Finisher, Hardwood Floor Finisher and Sander, Hardwood Floor Refinisher, Hardwood Floor Sander, Sander

## Summary

Scrape and sand wooden floors to smooth surfaces using floor scraper and floor sanding machine, and apply coats of finish.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 90 | Core | Buff and vacuum floors to ensure their cleanliness prior to the application of finish. |
| 88 | Core | Scrape and sand floor edges and areas inaccessible to floor sanders, using scrapers, disk-type sanders, and sandpaper. |
| 88 | Core | Inspect floors for smoothness. |
| 88 | Core | Attach sandpaper to rollers of sanding machines. |
| 87 | Core | Guide sanding machines over surfaces of floors until surfaces are smooth. |
| 86 | Core | Apply filler compound and coats of finish to floors to seal wood. |
| 66 | Core | Remove excess glue from joints, using knives, scrapers, or wood chisels. |
