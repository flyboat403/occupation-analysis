# 53-7021.00 - Crane and Tower Operators

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-7021.00
- Also called: Crane Operator, Heavy Equipment Operator, Machine Operator, Mobile Crane Operator, Overhead Crane Operator, Port Crane Operator, Scrap Crane Operator, Winchman, Woodyard Crane Operator

## Summary

Operate mechanical boom and cable or tower and cable equipment to lift and move materials, machines, or products in many directions.
