# 51-6031.00 - Sewing Machine Operators

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-6031.00
- Also called: Line Closer, Machine Operator, Prototype Sewer, Sample Maker, Seamer, Seamstress, Sew On Operator, Sewer, Sewing Machine Operator, Zipper Machine Operator

## Summary

Operate or tend sewing machines to join, reinforce, decorate, or perform related sewing operations in the manufacture of garment or nongarment products.
