# 53-7062.04 - Recycling and Reclamation Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-7062.04
- Also called: Bobcat Driver, Box Sorter, Convenience Recycle Center Technician (Convenience Recycle Center Tech), Deconstruction and Decontamination Waste Operations Specialist (D and D Waste Operations Specialist), Non-Ferrous Material Handler, Sort Line Worker, Sorter, Transfer Station Operator

## Summary

Prepare and sort materials or products for recycling. Identify and remove hazardous substances. Dismantle components of products such as appliances.

## Tasks

|------------|----------|------|
