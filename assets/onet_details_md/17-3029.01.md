# 17-3029.01 - Non-Destructive Testing Specialists

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 17-3029.01
- Also called: Industrial Radiographer, NDE Technician (Non-Destructive Evaluation Technician), NDT Coordinator (Non-Destructive Testing Coordinator), NDT Inspector (Non-Destructing Testing Inspector), NDT Specialist (Non-Destructive Testing Specialist), NDT Technical Specialist (Non-Destructive Testing Technical Specialist), NDT Technician (Non-Destructive Testing Technician)

## Summary

Test the safety of structures, vehicles, or vessels using x-ray, ultrasound, fiber optic or related equipment.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 94 | Core | Interpret the results of all methods of non-destructive testing (NDT), such as acoustic emission, electromagnetic, leak, liquid penetrant, magnetic particle, neutron radiographic, radiographic, thermal or infrared, ultrasonic, vibration analysis, and visual testing. |
