# 51-6041.00 - Shoe and Leather Workers and Repairers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 51-6041.00
- Also called: Boot Maker, Cobbler, Leather Worker, Saddle and Harness Maker, Shoe Cutter, Shoe Maker, Shoe Repairer, Shoe Repairman, Stitcher

## Summary

Construct, decorate, or repair leather and leather-like products, such as luggage, shoes, and saddles. May use hand tools.
