# 27-1013.00 - Fine Artists, Including Painters, Sculptors, and Illustrators

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-1013.00
- Also called: Artist, Automotive Artist, Blacksmith, Fine Artist, Ice Carver, Illustrator, Muralist, Painter, Portrait Artist, Sculptor

## Summary

Create original artwork using any of a wide variety of media and techniques.
