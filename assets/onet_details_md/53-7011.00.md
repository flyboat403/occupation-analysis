# 53-7011.00 - Conveyor Operators and Tenders

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-7011.00
- Also called: Chipper Operator, Debarker Operator, Flumer, Line Operator, Machine Operator, Package Line Operator, Packaging Line Operator, Packing Line Operator, Process Operator, Strapper Operator

## Summary

Control or tend conveyors or conveyor systems that move materials or products to and from stockpiles, processing stations, departments, or vehicles. May control speed and routing of materials or products.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 88 | Core | Inform supervisors of equipment malfunctions that need to be addressed. |
| 87 | Core | Observe conveyor operations and monitor lights, dials, and gauges to maintain specified operating levels and to detect equipment malfunctions. |
| 86 | Core | Record production data such as weights, types, quantities, and storage locations of materials, as well as equipment performance problems and downtime. |
