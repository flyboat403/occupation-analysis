# 39-5011.00 - Barbers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 39-5011.00
- Also called: Barber, Barber Shop Operator, Barber Stylist, Stylist

## Summary

Provide barbering services, such as cutting, trimming, shampooing, and styling hair; trimming beards; or giving shaves.
