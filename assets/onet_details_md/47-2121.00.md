# 47-2121.00 - Glaziers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-2121.00
- Also called: Auto Glass Tech (Automobile Glass Technician), Commercial Glazier, Field Glazier, Glass Installer, Glass Technician (Glass Tech), Glassman, Glazer, Glazier, Glazier Worker, Union Glazier

## Summary

Install glass in windows, skylights, store fronts, and display cases, or on surfaces, such as building fronts, interior walls, ceilings, and tabletops.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
