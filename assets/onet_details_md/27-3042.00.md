# 27-3042.00 - Technical Writers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-3042.00
- Also called: Documentation Designer, Documentation Specialist, Engineering Writer, Information Developer, Medical Writer, Narrative Writer, Requirements Analyst, Technical Communicator, Technical Writer

## Summary

Write technical materials, such as equipment manuals, appendices, or operating and maintenance instructions. May assist in layout work.
