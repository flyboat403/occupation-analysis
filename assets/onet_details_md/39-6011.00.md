# 39-6011.00 - Baggage Porters and Bellhops

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 39-6011.00
- Also called: Bell Captain, Bell Person, Bellhop, Bellman, Bellperson, Doorman, Ground Support Agent, Sky Cap, Skycap, Valet

## Summary

Handle baggage for travelers at transportation terminals or for guests at hotels or similar establishments.
