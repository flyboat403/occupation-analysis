# 29-1011.00 - Chiropractors

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 29-1011.00
- Also called: Chiropractic Doctor (DC), Chiropractic Neurologist, Chiropractic Physician, Chiropractor

## Summary

Assess, treat, and care for patients by manipulation of spine and musculoskeletal system. May provide spinal adjustment or address sacral or pelvic misalignment.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|

| 98 | Core | Diagnose health problems by reviewing patients' health and medical histories, questioning, observing, and examining patients and interpreting x-rays. |
