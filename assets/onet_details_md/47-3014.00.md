# 47-3014.00 - Helpers--Painters, Paperhangers, Plasterers, and Stucco Masons

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-3014.00
- Also called: Painter Helper, Plaster Helper, Plaster Tender

## Summary

Help painters, paperhangers, plasterers, or stucco masons by performing duties requiring less skill. Duties include using, supplying, or holding materials or tools, and cleaning work area and equipment.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 77 | Core | Clean work areas and equipment. |
| 74 | Core | Perform support duties to assist painters, paperhangers, plasterers, or masons. |

| 68 | Core | Erect scaffolding. |
| 67 | Core | Fill cracks or breaks in surfaces of plaster articles or areas with putty or epoxy compounds. |
| 62 | Core | Supply or hold tools and materials. |

| 41 | Supplemental | Pour specified amounts of chemical solutions into stripping tanks. |
