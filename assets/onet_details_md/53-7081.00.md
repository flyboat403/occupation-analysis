# 53-7081.00 - Refuse and Recyclable Material Collectors

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-7081.00
- Also called: Front Load Trash Truck Driver, Garbage Man, Recycle Driver, Refuse Collector, Roll Off Container Truck Driver, Roll Off Truck Driver, Sanitation Laborer, Swamper, Trash Collector, Truck Driver

## Summary

Collect and dump refuse or recyclable materials from containers into truck. May drive truck.
