# 11-3051.04 - Biomass Power Plant Managers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-3051.04
- Also called: Fuel Manager, Maintenance Manager, Maintenance Superintendent, Maintenance Supervisor, Operations and Maintenance Manager (O&amp;M Manager), Operations Superintendent, Operations Supervisor, Plant Manager, Utilities Superintendent

## Summary

Manage operations at biomass power generation facilities. Direct work activities at plant, including supervision of operations and maintenance staff.
