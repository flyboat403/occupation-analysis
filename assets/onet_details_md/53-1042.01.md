# 53-1042.01 - Recycling Coordinators

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-1042.01
- Also called: Heavy Equipment Supervisor, Recycle Coordinator, Recycling Coordinator, Recycling Manager, Recycling Program Manager, Recycling Specialist, Route Supervisor, Solid Waste Division Supervisor, Waste Reduction Coordinator

## Summary

Supervise curbside and drop-off recycling programs for municipal governments or private firms.
