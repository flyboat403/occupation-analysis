# 13-1071.00 - Human Resources Specialists

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 13-1071.00
- Also called: Corporate Recruiter, Employment Representative, HR Analyst (Human Resources Analyst), HR Coordinator (Human Resources Coordinator), HR Generalist (Human Resources Generalist), Human Resources Representative (HR Rep), Human Resources Specialist (HR Specialist), Personnel Analyst, Personnel Officer, Recruiter

## Summary

Recruit, screen, interview, or place individuals within an organization. May perform other activities in multiple human resources areas.
