# 35-3023.01 - Baristas

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 35-3023.01
- Also called: Barista, Catering Barista

## Summary

Prepare or serve specialty coffee or other beverages. Serve food such as baked goods or sandwiches to patrons.
