# 29-9099.01 - Midwives

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 29-9099.01
- Also called: Birth Center Midwife, Certified Direct-Entry Midwife, Certified Professional Midwife (CPM), Homebirth Midwife, Lay Midwife, Licensed and Certified Midwife, Licensed Certified Professional Midwife, Licensed Direct Entry Midwife, Licensed Midwife (LM)

## Summary

Provide prenatal care and childbirth assistance.
