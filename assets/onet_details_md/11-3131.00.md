# 11-3131.00 - Training and Development Managers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 11-3131.00
- Also called: Education and Development Manager, L and D Director (Learning and Development Director), Learning Manager, Organizational Development Manager (OD Manager), Staff Development Director, Staff Training and Development Manager, Training and Development Coordinator (T and D Coordinator), Training and Development Director (T and D Director), Training Director, Training Manager

## Summary

Plan, direct, or coordinate the training and development activities and staff of an organization.
