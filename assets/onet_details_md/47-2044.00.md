# 47-2044.00 - Tile and Stone Setters

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 47-2044.00
- Also called: Ceramic Tile Mechanic, Ceramic Tile Setter, Tile and Marble Installer, Tile and Marble Setter, Tile Finisher, Tile Installer, Tile Man, Tile Mason, Tile Mechanic, Tile Setter

## Summary

Apply hard tile, stone, and comparable materials to walls, floors, ceilings, countertops, and roof decks.
