# 49-9011.00 - Mechanical Door Repairers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 49-9011.00
- Also called: Commercial Door Installer, Commercial Installer, Door Installer, Door Technician, Garage Door Installer, Garage Door Technician, Installation Technician, Residential Door Installer, Service Technician

## Summary

Install, service, or repair automatic door mechanisms and hydraulic doors. Includes garage door mechanics.
