# 53-3051.00 - Bus Drivers, School

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-3051.00
- Also called: Bus Drive Coordinator, Bus Driver, CDL Bus Driver (Commercial Driver's License Bus Driver), Public School Bus Driver, School Bus Driver, School Van Driver, Shuttle Bus Driver, SPED Bus Driver (Special Education Bus Driver), SPED School Bus Driver (Special Education School Bus Driver), Student Driver

## Summary

Drive a school bus to transport students. Ensure adherence to safety rules. May assist students in boarding or exiting.
