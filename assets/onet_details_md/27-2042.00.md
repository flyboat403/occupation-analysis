# 27-2042.00 - Musicians and Singers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-2042.00
- Also called: Choir Member, Chorister, Gospel Singer, Musician, Opera Singer, Orchestra Musician, Percussionist, Singer, Violinist, Vocalist

## Summary

Play one or more musical instruments or sing. May perform on stage, for broadcasting, or for sound or video recording.
