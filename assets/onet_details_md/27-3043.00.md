# 27-3043.00 - Writers and Authors

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 27-3043.00
- Also called: Advertisement Agency Copywriter (Ad Agency Copywriter), Advertising Associate, Advertising Copywriter, Advertising Writer, Communications Specialist, Copywriter, Freelance Copywriter, Promotion Writer, Search Engine Optimization Copywriter (SEO Copywriter), Web Content Writer

## Summary

Originate and prepare written material, such as scripts, stories, advertisements, and other material.
