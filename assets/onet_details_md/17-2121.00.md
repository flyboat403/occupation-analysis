# 17-2121.00 - Marine Engineers and Naval Architects

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 17-2121.00
- Also called: Marine Architect, Marine Design Engineer, Marine Engineer, Marine Engineering Consultant, Marine Structural Designer, Marine Surveyor, Naval Architect, Naval Architect Specialist, Ship Equipment Engineer, Structural Engineer

## Summary

Design, develop, and evaluate the operation of marine vessels, ship machinery, and related equipment, such as power supply and propulsion systems.
