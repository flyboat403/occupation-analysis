# 41-9031.00 - Sales Engineers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 41-9031.00
- Also called: Business Development Engineer, Inside Sales Engineer, Product Sales Engineer, Sales Applications Engineer, Sales Engineer, Technical Marketing Engineer, Technical Sales Engineer

## Summary

Sell business goods or services, the selling of which requires a technical background equivalent to a baccalaureate degree in engineering.
