# 45-1011.00 - First-Line Supervisors of Farming, Fishing, and Forestry Workers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 45-1011.00
- Also called: Animal Research Facility Supervisor, Cattle Manager, Farm Supervisor, Fish Hatchery Manager, Harvesting Supervisor, Hatchery Manager, Logging Crew Foreman, Logging Supervisor, Pest Management Supervisor, Wildlife Manager

## Summary

Directly supervise and coordinate the activities of agricultural, forestry, aquacultural, and related workers.
