# 41-2022.00 - Parts Salespersons

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 41-2022.00
- Also called: Parts Advisor, Parts Consultant, Parts Counter Salesperson, Parts Counterman, Parts Counterperson, Parts Person, Parts Salesman, Parts Salesperson, Parts Specialist, Wholesale Parts Salesperson

## Summary

Sell spare and replacement parts and equipment in repair shop or parts store.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
