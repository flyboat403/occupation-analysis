# 17-1022.01 - Geodetic Surveyors

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 17-1022.01
- Also called: Geodesist, Geodetic Advisor, Geodetic Engineer, Geodetic Survey Director, Geodetic Surveyor, Land Surveyor, Licensed Land Surveyor

## Summary

Measure large areas of the Earth's surface using satellite observations, global navigation satellite systems (GNSS), light detection and ranging (LIDAR), or related sources.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
|------------|----------|------|
| 91 | Core | Analyze control or survey data to ensure adherence to project specifications or land survey standards. |
