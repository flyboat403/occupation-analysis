# 53-5031.00 - Ship Engineers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 53-5031.00
- Also called: Barge Engineer, Engineer, Ferry Engineer, Harbor Engineer, Port Engineer, Ship Engineer, Towboat Engineer, Tug Boat Engineer, Vessel Engineer

## Summary

Supervise and coordinate activities of crew engaged in operating and maintaining engines, boilers, deck machinery, and electrical, sanitary, and refrigeration equipment aboard ship.

## Tasks

| Importance | Category | Task |
