# 41-9011.00 - Demonstrators and Product Promoters

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 41-9011.00
- Also called: Brand Ambassador, Demo Specialist (Demonstration Specialist), Demonstrator, Event Specialist, Field Merchandiser, Food Demonstrator, In Store Demonstrator, Merchandiser, Product Ambassador, Product Demonstrator

## Summary

Demonstrate merchandise and answer questions for the purpose of creating public interest in buying the product. May sell demonstrated merchandise.
