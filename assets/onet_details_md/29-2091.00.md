# 29-2091.00 - Orthotists and Prosthetists

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 29-2091.00
- Also called: Certified Orthotist (CO), Certified Pedorthist, Certified Prosthetist (CP), Certified Prosthetist Orthotist (CPO), Licensed Orthotist, LPO (Licensed Prosthetist Orthotist), Orthotic Practitioner, Orthotist, Prosthetic Practitioner, Prosthetist

## Summary

Design, measure, fit, and adapt orthopedic braces, appliances or prostheses, such as limbs or facial parts for patients with disabling conditions.
