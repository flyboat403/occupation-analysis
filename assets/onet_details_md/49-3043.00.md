# 49-3043.00 - Rail Car Repairers

**Quick Facts:**
- SOC Code: 49-3043.00
- Also called: Freight Maintenance Specialist, Locomotive Repairman, Rail Car Maintenance Mechanic, Rail Car Mechanic, Rail Car Repairer, Rail Car Repairman, Rail Car Sandblaster, Rail Car Welder, Railroad Car Repairman, Train Car Repairman

## Summary

Diagnose, adjust, repair, or overhaul railroad rolling stock, mine cars, or mass transit rail cars.
